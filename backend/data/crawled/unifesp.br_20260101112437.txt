It became a federal institution in 1956 and a federal university in 1994. Now it holds seven campuses (Baixada Santista, Diadema, Guarulhos, Osasco, São José dos Campos, São Paulo and Zona Leste) with 55 undergraduate courses, 68 graduate programs, 1,600 faculty members, 3,700 employees and 20,300 regular students.
