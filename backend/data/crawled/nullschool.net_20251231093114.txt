Nullschool Technologies Inc. Nullschool Technologies Inc. Introduction Nullschool Technologies Inc. helps people understand Earth science through the visualization of weather
        and climate data. Our website, earth.nullschool.net , has been
        in operation since late 2013, providing access to real-time global wind, weather, ocean, and pollution
        conditions on an interactive animated map. Millions of visitors rely on earth.nullschool.net to understand
        the world around them. It has been featured in textbooks, scientific journals, museum exhibits,
        documentaries, magazines, and newscasts as an intuitive and innovative tool for understanding Earth's
        dynamic environment. Contact inquiries@nullschool.net for licensing or other
        questions.
