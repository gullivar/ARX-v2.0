L.L.Bean - The Outside Is Inside Everything We Make.
Founded in 1912 by Leon Leonwood Bean, L.L.Bean offers thousands of high-quality products at reasonable prices. All made to last and backed by our legendary customer service. Discover the comfort and classic styles of our Men's clothing, Women's clothing and Kids' clothing along with L.L.Bean Boots, shoes and slippers. Explore the outdoors with our lab- and field-tested outdoor gear for hunting, camping and fly fishing, including everything from tents, backpacks and sleeping bags. No matter what we sell, from flattering swimwear and rain jackets to our finely crafted furniture, soft, colorful bedding and innovative luggage, you can count on L.L.Bean for quality and value every day.
