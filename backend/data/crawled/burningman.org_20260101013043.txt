Burning Man is Built by YOU

Burning Man is not something you attend — it’s something you do. All the ways you participate are truly awe-inspiring, sparking a more creative, connected world. Your support for Burning Man Project funds Black Rock City Honoraria art, makes tickets accessible to more people, and nurtures Burning Man in communities everywhere.
