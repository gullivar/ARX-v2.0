Log everything from device events to network context if it might help one day, capture it now. 03 Builds intelligence into devices Devices decide what to log and store in real time powered by a local Ring Buffer and smart logic at the edge. 04 Has instant feedback cycles Make telemetry changes and deploy them instantly. No need to wait for releases or approvals. 05 Gives mobile the focus it deserves Mobile devs finally get first-class observability on par with backend tools, purpose-built for app fleets. 01 Expensive & inefficient Whether you pay for storage or per host, Old Observability costs a lot: well past 30% of most teams' entire infrastructure bill. That's because you pay for what you generate, instead of what you actually use. 02 Forces teams to log less High costs make teams log only what's essential, leading to gaps in context when things break. 03 Assumes dumb devices Old Observability treats devices as passive log shippers with no logic beyond basic filters or hardcoded telemetry. 04 Has slow feedback cycles Lack of context forces teams to add logs after issues occur, delaying fixes until the next deployment and new data collection. 05 Ignores mobile Old Observability leaves mobile behind. Devs rely on patchwork tools and only hear about bugs when users report them. Log everything, but only send what you really need Most solutions start streaming data to expensive backend observability pipelines on install. Instead, we believe you shouldn't be limited by log limits and hefty overage fees. We use a local storage ring buffer combined with our control plane to give you maximum control over the data you need, anytime you need it.
