As per the directives from Hon. High Court of Kerala, it is being brought to the urgent attention and strict compliance of all Sabarimala Pilgrims that the use and carriage of plastic materials such as bottles, sachets, wraps, and covers in the Irumudi is strictly prohibited.
As per the directive from Hon'ble High Court of Kerala, the sale and usage of synthetic non bio-degradable, non food-grade, chemical dyes and colour powders and Kumkum, containing substances that are widely harmful for the environment, organisms and people is prohibited and should not be brought by pilgrims either to Sannidhanam or to Erumely, where the ritual of Petta Thullal is performed.
This is to inform that Travancore Devaswom Board has not authorised anyone to install an Idol of Lord Ayyappa for worship, in the premises of Sabarimala Sree Dharma Sastha Temple, or permitted anyone to collect money from the public for this purpose.
Pilgrims are hereby informed that there is NO change to the timings of Civil Darshan at Sannidhanam as of now. Kindly ignore any fake messages informing otherwise.
