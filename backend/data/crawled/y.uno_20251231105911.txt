HAVE QUESTIONS? Frequently asked questions and answers Find answers to common questions about payment solutions and how Yuno can address your specific business needs. What is payment orchestration, and how does it work? Payment orchestration is a technology layer that simplifies and centralizes the management of all your payment infrastructure. Instead of integrating each payment provider, method, fraud tool, and acquirer separately, orchestration connects them all through a single API. This allows you to intelligently route transactions, reduce technical overhead, and adapt quickly to new markets or payment preferences. Yuno’s platform orchestrates the full payment lifecycle – from checkout to authorization, routing, fraud prevention, settlement, and reconciliation – enabling better performance, more flexibility, and global scalability. Why do global companies need payment orchestration? As companies expand across borders, the complexity of managing payments multiplies. Each region has its own preferred payment methods, regulatory requirements, fraud risks, and performance benchmarks. Without orchestration, managing these factors individually becomes costly, slow, and inefficient. Yuno allows businesses to localize payment experiences in each market without building everything from scratch. From supporting 1,000+ payment methods across 200 countries to ensuring compliance and optimizing routing logic, orchestration is essential for reducing friction, increasing conversions, and growing revenue globally. How can Yuno help increase my approval rates and reduce failed payments? Approval rates often suffer when transactions are routed to underperforming processors or when payment gateways have latency or reliability issues. Yuno solves this through Smart Routing – a feature that automatically sends each transaction to the optimal processor based on real-time performance data, geography, and cost. This dynamic approach minimizes declines and ensures more payments get approved. Yuno clients have seen up to a 7% uplift in approval rates and significant recovery of failed transactions, especially in high-volume, multi-market environments. Is it easy to add new payment methods or providers with Yuno? Yes. Yuno was built to eliminate the technical complexity of adding new providers. Instead of long development cycles, merchants can enable or disable payment methods with just a few clicks using our no-code interface. With over 1,000 payment methods already integrated, including local cards, wallets, bank transfers, BNPL, and more, you can go live in new markets in weeks instead of months – without rewriting code or managing separate contracts for every provider. How does Yuno protect against payment fraud and chargebacks? Yuno’s orchestration platform includes robust fraud prevention tools that help you stay protected without adding friction to the user experience. You can integrate fraud detection providers with one click, use tokenization to protect customer data, and enable 3D Secure authentication for high-risk payments. Our system also allows you to customize fraud rules by geography, risk level, or transaction type. The goal is to balance security with performance, reducing chargebacks and false declines while maintaining high approval rates.
