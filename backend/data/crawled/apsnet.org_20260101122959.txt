The virtual issue ​ offers a compelling look at the research—both widely cited and quietly influential—that laid the groundwork for scientific breakthroughs across generations. ​ In addition to the scientific articles, the virtual issue features personal reflections from many of the original authors. These narratives provide behind-the-scenes glimpses into the origins of key ideas, collaborations, and challenges—underscoring the human side of scientific discovery. Senior editors also contributed short commentaries on their favorite papers, offering insights into how these works shaped their own careers and the broader field.
