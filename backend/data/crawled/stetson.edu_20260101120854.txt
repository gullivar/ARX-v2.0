LOCATIONS

You should know that Central Florida holds the title of being the #1 tourist destination in the world. You'll find Stetson perfectly situated in the heart of this amazing place, surrounded by attractions, shopping, dining, sunshine, and some of the world's finest beaches.

If you visit, you'll discover Stetson's historic campus in DeLand, Florida. If you're interested in the College of Law, head to Gulfport, Florida, or check out the Law Center in Tampa, Florida. Why don't you take some time to explore the campus? You can also go on a virtual tour to see more of what we have to offer.
