Joe's Pizza: A New York City Slice Icon

Joe's Pizza has been serving up classic New York slices since 1975, earning a reputation as one of the city's most beloved pizzerias. Located in Greenwich Village, Joe's is famous for its thin, foldable crust, tangy tomato sauce, and perfectly melted cheese.

The shop's no-frills atmosphere attracts everyone from locals and students to celebrities and tourists. The secret to Joe's enduring success is simple: fresh ingredients, time-tested technique, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

If you're looking for the quintessential New York slice experience, Joe's Pizza is a non-negotiable stop on your culinary tour of the city.
