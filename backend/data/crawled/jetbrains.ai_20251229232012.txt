Secure and flexible AI solutions on your terms
Responsible use of intellectual property

Proprietary models are trained solely on public, permissively licensed code. The models are designed to respect intellectual property rights and ensure compliance with all applicable legal and ethical standards.

Data retention and compliance

JetBrains does not persist customer data unless users explicitly opt in to detailed data collection, which is used exclusively to improve product quality. No data is retained by third-party providers.

Full data and model control

With on-premises solutions, you have complete control over your data and model management to ensure it conforms to your company's security, privacy, and compliance standards.
