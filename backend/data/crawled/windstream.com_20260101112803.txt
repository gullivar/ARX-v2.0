your questions, answered
How do I move my Kinetic service?

It’s easy to move your internet, TV or phone service to a new residential address. You can also get a deal on bundles. Even if you’re a current customer, you can enjoy new customer pricing for your new home, with free activation and installation. To discuss a service transfer, call 855-737-1642.

What is Kinetic?

Kinetic offers residential fiber internet with all the speed, reliability and security you need at home, plus capabilities like voice.

What products does Kinetic offer?

Kinetic offers products for residential connectivity. Products include TV and entertainment, security, voice, Wi-Fi and home security.

Where is Kinetic offered?

While Kinetic continues to grow, we currently provide residential service in 18 states: Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Iowa, Kentucky, Minnesota, Mississippi, Missouri, Nebraska, New Mexico, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, South Carolina and Texas.
