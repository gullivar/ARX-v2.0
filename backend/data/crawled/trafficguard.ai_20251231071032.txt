Winning at Protecting Your Digital Ad Spend FAQs of Click Fraud Can’t find what you are looking for? Go to our support page to learn more. Get Support What is click fraud in digital advertising? Click fraud is a form of advertising fraud where fake or malicious clicks are generated on paid ads to drain budgets or manipulate performance data. These fraudulent clicks can come from bots, click farms, competitors, or even misattributed users. Understanding the meaning of click fraud helps advertisers spot the difference between genuine engagement and invalid traffic that delivers no return. What are the common types of click fraud? There are several types of click fraud affecting modern ad campaigns. These include bot traffic that mimics human behaviour, incentivised clicks from users paid to engage, click farms generating fake activity, and competitor sabotage aimed at exhausting budgets. Other forms of advertising fraud include cookie stuffing, postback manipulation, and bonus abuse in affiliate marketing. Early detection of these tactics is crucial to sustaining performance and preventing wasted spend. How does click fraud affect PPC campaigns? Click fraud damages PPC campaign performance by inflating click volumes without generating conversions. Invalid clicks distort key metrics such as CTR, CPC, and conversion rates, leading to poor optimisation decisions. Over time, this malicious activity can reduce visibility, waste valuable ad spend, and block campaigns from reaching real users. Accurate detection and click fraud protection software ensure your PPC campaigns stay efficient and profitable. What are the signs of click fraud? Advertisers can identify suspicious activity by monitoring sudden spikes in clicks, poor conversion ratios, or traffic from fake or irrelevant geographies. Other warning signs include short session durations, high bounce rates, and repetitive clicks from the same IP or device. These red flags indicate fraudulent activities from bots or bad actors. Using real-time analytics tools such as TrafficGuard helps you detect these patterns before they cause major budget losses. How can I detect click fraud in my campaigns? Detecting click fraud requires advanced fraud detection tools that analyse engagement data in real time. Manual reviews often miss patterns of invalid traffic, while automated software continuously monitors traffic quality and identifies fraudulent clicks before they distort your reports. The cost of click fraud extends beyond wasted ad spend. Fraudulent activity skews marketing data, increases acquisition costs, and reduces your return on ad spend (ROAS).
