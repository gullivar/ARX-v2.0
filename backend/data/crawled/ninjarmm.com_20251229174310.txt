Automated Endpoint Management Platform
Endpoint Management

Unify visibility and control and automate the most tedious IT tasks with NinjaOne’s easy-to-use, all-in-one endpoint solution. Monitor, support, and control your endpoints regardless of operating system from smartphones to servers. Minimize downtime and drive employee productivity. More about Endpoint Management

Remote Monitoring & Management

Drive efficiency through out-of-the box automation and deliver the best end-user experience with cloud-native NinjaOne. The No. 1 RMM solution enables policy-based management, proactive visibility and alerting, and more. Provide quick and effective service to your customers. More about RMM

Mobile Device Management

Manage Android and Apple mobile devices across all your operating systems — all within a single, intuitive console. NinjaOne MDM’s inventory tracking, easy provisioning, configuration management, and remote troubleshooting ensure your users remain secure and productive on the go. More about MDM

Patch Management

Secure remote and hybrid endpoints with automated, cross-OS patch management. Improve zero-days and problem patch management with granular control backed by CVE/CVSS alerts and scores. Deliver patches to any connected endpoint regardless of location through cloud-based, agent patch delivery. More about Patch Management

Backup

NinjaOne Backup is a cloud-native backup solution built for ransomware recovery. Backups are encrypted in the cloud, locally and in transit, requiring re-authentication, MFA, and confirmation of any deletions. Use it how you need it across any Windows or Mac OS, file & folder, and/or image. More about Backup
