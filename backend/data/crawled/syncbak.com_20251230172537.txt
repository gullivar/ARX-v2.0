OUR COMPANY

Founded in 2009 by visionary CEO Jack Perry, Zeam Media has been leading the charge in OTT innovation, powering live for industry giants such as Paramount+, Hulu, fuboTV, the NFL, Amazon, Roku, and Apple. Zeam Media represents the confluence of 'Media Mogul,' our streaming technology, and 'Ad Sync,' our advertising platform, creating a seamless content and ad integration across a multitude of streams.

Zeam embodies the next leap in streaming, delivering compelling hyper-local and original content. More than just entertainment, we are the beacon for local stories, offering everyone, everywhere, free access to the narratives that resonate most with our audience. Zeam is where innovation meets community, transforming the way you connect with content nationwide.
