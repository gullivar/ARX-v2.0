Learn more about who we are scroll to content © Monirul Bhuiyan/AFP/Getty How we work Every year, the Open Society Foundations give grants to a diverse array of groups and individuals who promote our values—through a unique network that is guided by local voices and global expertise. $24.2B Total expenditures Over the past three decades, our giving has focused on supporting people who are trying to make their communities fairer, freer, and more harmonious. $1.2B 2024 expenditures Our approach involves focused, time-bound funding interventions, long-term support for partners, and flexible funds to respond to sudden crises. 50,000+ Total Grants Awarded Our grants—ranging from a few thousand to hundreds of thousands of dollars—are awarded to organizations and individuals in scores of countries around the world.
