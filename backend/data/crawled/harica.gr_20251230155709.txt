SECURITY & TRUST
Why should you trust HARICA?
HARICA is currently the only "Root CA Operator" in Greece that participates in all major Global "ROOT CA" Trust Programs (Adobe, Apple, Microsoft, Mozilla), and operates as a "Trust Anchor" in widely used Application Software and Operating Systems (Adobe, Apple, Google, Microsoft, Mozilla, Linux).

It has received a successful Conformance Assessment Report fulfilling the requirements of Regulation (EU) 910/2014 (also known as eIDAS) in the areas of "Qualified" electronic Signatures, Seals and Time Stamps. This Certification ensures that HARICA's Certificates for Qualified eSignatures/eSeals are legally binding in Greece, in all European Union Member States, and non-EU Countries that have a bi-lateral agreement with the European Union.
