MP3Juice – Free MP3 Search Engine is the easiest way to search for your favorite music across multiple sources and download it as MP3 (audio) or MP4 (video) files for free. By using our search engine, you accept our Terms of Use.

How to use MP3Juice?
Simply type or paste your search query above and click the „Search” button.
As soon as we find matching results, you will receive a result list. Please note: The first search will always contain results from one source. If you would like to extend the search to another source, click the „Load more” button at the end of the result list.
If you find the right result, simply click one of the „Download” buttons. Depending on the source, the download may start immediately, or the file may need to be converted to the selected format before it can be downloaded. This may take a short while.
