No Credit Card Required. FAQs Answers to some of life's greatest questions found here assist How do I start a blog on Showit? 1. Design your blog pages in Showit...both your "Post List" and your "Single Post" pages. 2. Request a blog setup with our Support Team . 3. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard that's connected to your Showit site. 4. Blog like a boss. Read More About Setting Up Your Blog Pages and Blogging in Showit How does SEO work with Showit? SEO with Showit works just like it does on any other platform—the real magic comes from solid content, quality backlinks, and a well-structured site. Showit gives you the tools to set things up the way search engines love, so you're free to focus on your strategy without your website holding you back. Read more about SEO and Showit here. Can I use my current domain? Absolutely! You can use your current domain and keep it hosted wherever you choose. You will simply point your DNS records to our servers to display your site. Check out this doc for more info on Showit hosting. How do I get help with my Showit site? Showit Support is here for you 24/7, with lightning-fast responses during business hours (7am–7pm MST, Monday through Friday). Just click the chat bubble on our website, Help Docs, or right inside the app to reach out. Most questions get a reply in under an hour—and if your site’s down, we’re on it immediately. How do I set up a store on my Showit site? To start selling with Showit, just pick a shopping cart platform you love—like Shopify, WooCommerce, or ThriveCart—and add your products there. Connect it to a payment processor like Stripe or PayPal, then integrate everything into your Showit site. Once it’s all set up, you're ready to start selling! Read more about building a store here. How do I set up a contact form on my Showit site? To create a form in Showit, just use text elements for each field and check the "Enable Input" box in the text properties to choose your field type. Add a text box to act as your submit button, then set it up in the click actions panel. You’ll also want two canvas views—one for entering info and another for your “form submitted” message. Read more about contact forms here. What integrations work with Showit? All your favorite tools—like calendar sign-ups, memberships, online courses, ConvertKit, HoneyBook, BDOW!, and more—play nicely with Showit. Most integrations work through simple HTML embeds or WordPress plugins. Basically, if it works with WordPress or can be embedded, you can make it work with Showit too! Learn more about integrations with Showit here. Which pricing plan is the best for me? Showit offers flexible plans so your site can grow right alongside your business. Just need a website without a blog? The Basic plan is perfect. Ready to start blogging? Go with the Blog Starter plan. And if you're migrating an existing blog or need special plugins, the Advanced Blog plan has you covered. See our pricing here.
