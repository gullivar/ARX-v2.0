Macmillan

Macmillan Publishers is a leading publishing company and home to some of the world’s most cherished authors and creators. Macmillan’s imprints publish a wide range of genres and formats for every kind of reader — from adult fiction and nonfiction to many inspired children’s books. As a part of Holtzbrinck Publishing Group, a large family-owned media company headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, Macmillan Publishers is firmly committed to our employees, authors, and core values.
