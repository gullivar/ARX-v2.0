How to download a YouTube video?
Go to YouTube and search for a video you would like to download. Copy the video URL from your browser’s address bar. Please note: A valid YouTube video URL should look like this: youtube.com/watch?v=videoId.
Paste the video URL into the converter above and choose your preferred download format. You can select either MP3 or MP4. Click the „Convert” button.
The conversion process will start. Depending on the length of the video, this may take several minutes. As soon as the conversion is successfully completed, you can download the converted file by clicking the „Download” button.
