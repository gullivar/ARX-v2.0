Come Home to Houghton

Our beautiful waterfront campus in Michigan's Upper Peninsula is situated just miles from Lake Superior. Find yourself in a close-knit college town where winters are epic and outdoor adventure awaits. At Tech, we embrace our size, climate, sense of adventure, and originality.

Visit us in Houghton and find where you belong.

Chang K. Park Center for Student Wellness

The Chang K. Park Center for Student Wellness, located between the Memorial Union and Administration buildings, will advance student emotional, physical, and mental health by expanding campus capacity for integrated care, strengthening prevention and awareness efforts.

Congratulations to all of our graduates Huskies! Leading Scholar

The Leading Scholar Award is Michigan Tech’s premier scholarship. It is awarded to incoming first-year students who have not only excelled in their academic pursuits but also demonstrated outstanding leadership and a passion for serving others.
