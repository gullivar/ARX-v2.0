Twitter Trends: Understanding Social Media's Pulse

Twitter trends represent the collective voice of millions discussing current events, viral content, and breaking news in real-time. Since 2008, trending topics have become a crucial barometer for public opinion worldwide.

Instalker provides access to trends from 13+ countries, allowing users to compare global conversations. The trending algorithm considers volume, velocity, location, and engagement to surface the most talked-about topics.
