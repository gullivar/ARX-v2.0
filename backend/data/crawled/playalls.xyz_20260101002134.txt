Frequently Asked Questions
What video and audio formats does PlayAll support?

PlayAll supports all major video (MKV, MP4, FLV, etc.) and audio (MP3, M4A, etc.) formats. No extra plugins are needed for smooth playback.

How do I use floating window and background play?

While playing a video or music, tap the floating window button to enable picture-in-picture mode. You can continue enjoying your content while using other apps. Background play keeps your audio running even when the screen is off or you switch apps.

Can PlayAll convert video to audio?

Yes. PlayAll has a built-in audio extraction feature. You can convert any local video to a high-quality audio file with one tap.

How do I create and manage playlists?

In the music player, long-press or tap the menu to add songs to a new or existing playlist. Easily organize your music collection with PlayAll.
