What is Dynamic DNS or DDNS?

Dynamic DNS is a way for you to create DNS hostnames that are always up to date with your current IP. Do you host a server and hate having to remember your IP address? With Dynamic DNS you can create easy to remember hostnames that automatically update with your IP address so you can access your devices from anywhere in the world, whenever you want, and never need to remember an IP again!
