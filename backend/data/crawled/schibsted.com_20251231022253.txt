That’s our business. A Nordic media house built on trust What began as a small publishing house in 1839 is now a media house with some of the strongest news brands in the Nordics. Each day millions of  Swedes, Norwegians and Finns interact with Schibsted to be updated, engaged and entertained. On 8 June 2024 we became a stand-alone media company, separated from the marketplace and growth and investment divisions of Schibsted.
