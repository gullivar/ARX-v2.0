Your 2026 Noble Beach Prize Winners

The Noble Beach Prize honors top resorts in the Caribbean, Mexico and Hawaii for their chic, beachy vibes. Resorts enter in 13 categories, with CheapCaribbean judges picking finalists and external judges selecting winners. Here's to these dreamy resorts and unforgettable vacays!

The View–Priceless

Secrets Baby Beach Aruba takes the title for The View–Priceless. Soak in Aruba’s iconic turquoise waters from a serene adults-only escape where every angle is screensaver-worthy.

Learn More
The Bombest Bar

Secrets Tides Punta Cana takes the win for The Bombest Bar. Sip beachy cocktails, chill poolside and vibe out at the bar that brings the perfect mix of mellow and lively.

Learn More
Best Beach Bliss

The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa earns our crown for Best Beach Bliss. Kick back on Seven Mile Beach’s soft white sands and savor the kind of calm every True Beach Lover dreams about.

Learn More
Dreamiest "I Do"
Zoëtry Marigot Bay St. Lucia, shines as our winner for Dreamiest “I Do.” Say “I do” surrounded by lush hillsides, sparkling blue water and the most romantic views in St. Lucia.

Most Picture-Perfect Paradise

Secrets Cap Cana Resort & Spa stands out as our Most Picture-Perfect Paradise. Think powder-soft beaches, sparkling waters and a backdrop made for your most brag-worthy beach pics.

Learn More
Best Beach-O-Tainment
Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Punta Cana rocks its way to the top for Best Beach-O-Tainment. Turn up the fun with live music, epic entertainment and all-inclusive energy right on Punta Cana’s famous sands.
Learn More
Best Kid-Tivities

Nickelodeon Hotels & Resorts Punta Cana wins big for Best Kid-tivities. Slime, splash and play your way through endless family fun, because big smiles are part of the all-inclusive plan.

 

Learn More
Most Divine Dining
UNICO 20°87° Hotel Riviera Maya is our winner for Most Divine Dining. Taste your way through chef-driven menus, fresh local flavors and beachy bites worth craving long after vacay ends.

The Pool That Rules

Live Aqua Cancun is our clear standout for The Pool That Rules. Drift between Insta-worthy pools, chill vibes and all-inclusive indulgence in the heart of Cancun.

Learn More
Most SPAwesome Experience

Cayo Levantado Resort tops the list for Most SPAwesome Experience. Treat yourself to island-inspired wellness, soothing spa rituals and the ultimate recharge in a private-island paradise.

Learn More
The Sweetest Suite
Ventus Ha' at Marina El Cid Spa & Beach Resort-All Inclusive claims the prize for The Sweetest Suite. Spread out, slow down and live your best life with spacious suites designed for max comfort.
Learn More
Ultimate Romantic Retreat

El Dorado Casitas Royale steals our hearts as the Ultimate Romantic Retreat. Escape to dreamy casitas, private beach moments and the perfect setting for reconnecting.

Ultimate Island Vibe

Wailea Beach Resort-Marriott, Maui captures our pick for Ultimate Island Vibe. Live the laid-back Maui life with ocean views, breezy palms and sunsets that hit different every night.
