Register now! Respiratory sleep certified training programme - register now Registrations for the newly updated Respiratory sleep certified training programme are now open. This two-part programme aims to train healthcare professionals at post-specialty or postgraduate level in respiratory sleep medicine. The programme should be completed within two years and participants must successfully complete part one to progress to part two.
