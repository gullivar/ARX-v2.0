Our recent awards and prizes

The outstanding work of our journalists has been recognized with numerous awards. These include 59 Pulitzer Prizes, more than any other news organization in the categories for which we can compete.

2024 Pulitzer Prize

In 2024, we won the Pulitzer Prize for Feature Photography for our compelling, multi-country photojournalism showing migration from Central America to the United States.

2024 Academy Awards

“20 Days in Mariupol,” the feature documentary from The Associated Press and PBS documentary series Frontline won the Oscar for Best Documentary Feature Film at the 96th Academy Awards®.

2024 BAFTA film awards

In 2024, “20 Days in Mariupol,” the feature documentary from The Associated Press and PBS “Frontline” earned a 2024 EE BAFTA Film Award in the Best Documentary category.
