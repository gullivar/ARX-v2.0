Get in touch About Vusion Solutions Transform your shelves into smart, connected assets. VusionGroup’s Electronic Shelf Labels (ESLs) revolutionize in-store operations by connecting every product, shelf, and price to the cloud in real time. Our ESLs combine secure wireless communication and seamless integration with retail systems to ensure instant price updates, perfect accuracy, and operational efficiency. Their ultra-low-energy displays can include colors and multiple layers of information. More than a digital label, each ESL becomes part of a powerful Retail IoT ecosystem , enabling data-driven insights, real-time stock monitoring, and enhanced shopper engagement — creating truly connected, intelligent stores .
