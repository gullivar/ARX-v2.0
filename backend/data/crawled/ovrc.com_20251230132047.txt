Peace of Mind for Professionals and Their Clients

OvrC (oversee) is a revolutionary support platform for professionals that allows them to remotely manage, configure, and troubleshoot devices on the network. From basic home theaters to large-scale networking infrastructures, OvrC empowers pros to keep everything running smoothly.


Proactive Support

The network is always a priority, and now pros can keep an eye out for slow internet speeds or malfunctioning devices. Usually, they’ll solve issues before clients even notice there’s a problem.

Fast, Discreet Remote Service

Glitches happen. If a system starts exhibiting problems, pros can troubleshoot offline devices and solve issues quickly.


Personalized Mobile App

Through a customer mobile app configured by their pro, clients have the option to fix common issues and manage the network at their own convenience.


Remote Firmware Updates

OvrC notifies pros when the newest software updates are available, so they can solve problems and upgrade devices remotely.


Trusted by Pros

Pros worldwide rely on OvrC to keep their clients’ devices up and running.
