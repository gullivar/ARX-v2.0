Boost Mission development of high quality, expert reviewed, legally unencumbered, open-source libraries, inspiring standard enhancements, and advancing and disseminating software development best practices. It does this by fostering community engagement, nurturing leaders, providing necessary financial/legal support, and making directional decisions in the event of Boost community deadlock. Equally important to our mission is the guidance provided by our shared values. These are transparency, inclusivity, consensus-building, federated authorship, and community-driven leadership. 10M+ Total Downloads 165+ Individual Libraries Why Use Boost? In a word, Productivity . Use of high-quality libraries like Boost speeds initial development, results in fewer bugs, reduces reinvention-of-the-wheel, and cuts long-term maintenance costs.
