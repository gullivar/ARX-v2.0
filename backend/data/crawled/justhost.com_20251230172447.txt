FAQs

WHERE DO I LOG IN?

The login process won’t change much. Simply open our website, click the Login button, and use your current credentials to log into your account. To learn more, please see How To Login to Account Manager.

WILL MY RENEWAL DATES OR BILLING CYCLES CHANGE?

No, your renewal dates and billing cycles will not change, and you will still be able to update renewal settings at any time. Please see How to Use the Renewal Center to learn more.

WILL MY SERVER CHANGE (MIGRATION)?

No, your hosting server and content will not move.

DO I NEED TO MAKE ANY CHANGES ON MY END TO ENSURE A SMOOTH TRANSITION? 

No, all changes and settings will automatically update on our end.

IS MY SUPPORT CHANGING?

No, you will still have access to our support team. See our Contact Us page to chat or find our phone number.

WHERE DO I LOG INTO WEBMAIL​​​​​?

To learn more about logging into webmail, please see How To Access Webmail: Log into Your Email Account.

HOW DO I ACCESS MY PAYMENT AND BILLING INFORMATION?

To review your payment history and receipts, please see View Billing Receipts and Payment History.

HOW DO I NAVIGATE THE NEW HOME PAGE?

The new home page and user interface are designed intuitively for ease of use. You can review User Experience: New vs Old View Side-by-Side Comparison to learn more about the new navigation and features. 

WILL ANYTHING BE CHANGED WITH MY DOMAIN NAME DURING AND AFTER THE MIGRATION?

As apart of our consolidation efforts we are also updating our registrar name to Bluehost. This will have zero impact on your domain and requires zero changes from you. Going forward if a WHOIS/RDAP query is ran you will see the Registrar URL: updated to reflect www.bluehost.com tas well as the Registrar name now Bluehost Inc.
