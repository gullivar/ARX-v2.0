Faq
Who’s behind Tiny Astro?
Tiny Astro consists of a team of 6 full-time individuals broken down by engineers, product designers, and marketers. Both of the co-founders are also Forbes 30 under 30 alumni who scaled their Web2 platform to 5 million users and $100M in revenue.
How can I get access to the NFT analytics and other features?
You get full access to Astro Analytics (our analytics platform) if you own a Tiny Astro Genesis NFT.

Alternatively you can also subscribe to our monthly or quarterly plans here.
Why should I choose Tiny Astro over other tools?
The Tiny Astro team has over a decade of experience in building platforms that have scaled to millions of users. Our engineering and design team is unparalleled in their abilities and we are laser-focused on building a product that is intuitive and extremely easy to use.

Aside from having one of the fastest reveal speeds for any NFT tool in the market, we also provide a full suite of products to help you discover and trade NFTs with ease and confidence.
What do Tiny Astro NFT holders get?
As a Tiny Astro (TA) holder, you get:


Full access to Astro Analytics
Daily earnings of $ASTRO token when you nest your NFT
Ability to rent out access to Astro Analytics for friends and family
Access to our Astro Marketplace which allows you to enter giveaways, follow alpha callers, unlock new features and add-ons (sweeping, sniping bot + more)
