Many land managers are focused on fuels management projects, including prescribed burning, throughout the fall. Wildland fire agencies apply a variety of fuels management techniques to reduce the amount of available fuel (vegetation) that fuels unwanted wildfires. By proactively removing fuel, fire agencies are better protecting local communities from risk of wildfire.
