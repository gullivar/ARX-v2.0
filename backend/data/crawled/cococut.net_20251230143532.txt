How to Use Our Video Downloader Extension

CocoCut is a versatile video downloader extension that supports saving video and music files in various formats. Whether it is an MP4, MP3, AAC, or any other format, CocoCut can help you save it with ease.

Saving Video and Music Files
Click on the CocoCut extension icon located at your browser's toolbar.
A file list will appear.
Locate the desired file and click on the "download" icon to start the save process.
Saving Streaming Videos (HLS/M3U8)

CocoCut also allows you to save streaming videos, specifically those in HLS/M3U8 format.

Open the playback page of the streaming video you want to save.
Once the video is detected, CocoCut will display a number indicating its availability.
Click on the CocoCut extension icon at your browser's toolbar.
A file list will appear, presenting all available video options.
Locate the desired video and click on the "save" icon to start the save process.
Save Progress and Controls

When a video save is initiated, it will open in a new tab. You will be able to monitor the save progress and control it using the provided options.

Please note that once the save starts, you can close the video playback page, but the save page must be kept open for the process to continue.
