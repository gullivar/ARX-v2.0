Frequently Asked, Clearly Answered What is a performance marketing platform? Compared to traditional advertising, performance marketing facilitates the measuring of results, efficiently. This enables advertisers to know the real value that they get from their ad spend. With a performance marketing platform , advertisers have the flexibility of targeting particular segments, as well as monitoring the achievement of campaigns, which enhances efficient usage of resources while catering to specific target audiences. What are some examples of performance marketing? Performance marketing is where users pay based on the performance of a campaign. For instance, users might get charged each time a consumer clicks on a particular ad (which is referred to as pay per click) or, each time they make a sale. In a nut shell, performance marketing is the complete reverse of the conventional style of marketing and much more cost effective as well. How does Trackier help improve campaign performance? Trackier offers a sophisticated attribution model to provide our users with the real picture on the effectiveness of the marketing channel they are using. Trackier’s platform enables users to identify which touchpoints are the major ones and will help them effectively decide how to allocate available funds and, thus, achieve the highest possible ROI. Trackier’s affiliate tracking software helps you monitor partner performance, optimize campaigns, and scale your revenue channels with clarity.
