*Insperity HR360 promotional credit opportunity
A promotional credit of up to $500 per full-time employee is available for new Insperity clients who, between December 5, 2025, and January 31, 2026, sign a client service agreement (CSA) for co-employment services (the Insperity® HR360 solution or Insperity® HR360 Select Edition) and the applicable promotional credit CSA addendum. To be eligible, new clients must have a minimum of five full-time employees as of the first payroll pay date and the service effective date+ must occur between January 1, 2026, and March 31, 2026. Each new client is only eligible for one promotional credit. Terms and conditions apply as set forth in the required CSA addendum. See your Business Performance Advisor for more information.
+The service effective date is the first day of the first payroll period processed by Insperity.
The terms and conditions of the applicable promotional credit CSA addendum apply and shall control over any inconsistent or conflicting information.
