The Best-Run Businesses Choose Liongard!
Unmatched IT Visibility, Automated Security and Compliance at Scale

Gain complete control over your clients' IT environments with automated visibility across every asset, configuration, and system. Liongard gives you a centralized, comprehensive view of your entire stack, allowing you to eliminate blind spots, identify misconfigurations, and monitor every change with precision.  Give your clients peace of mind knowing that their business is secure, optimized, resilient and in compliance with cyber insurance requirements.

Asset Discovery and Inventory

Discovery and inventory all IT assets - identities, devices, software, and data, across cloud, on-prem, and endpoints. Gain full visibility to eliminate blind spots and fully understand the breadth of the attack surface.

Advanced Automated Documentation

Streamline documentation ensuring accurate, up-to-date records. Simplifying tracking, enhancing alignment with security best practices, and reduces manual entry and ticket resolution time.

Configuration Change Detection and Auditing

Monitor configurations for changes and deviations from best practice state, triggering alerts when action is needed. Empowering MSPs to take the necessary remediation steps, ensuring compliance and security.

Real-Time Monitoring & Remediation

Real-time monitoring provides instant user visibility, ensuring accurate inventory and compliance. Automated detection and remediation reduce security risks, cut manual effort, and enables holistic Attack Surface Management.
