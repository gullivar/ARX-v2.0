Opensignal delivers unique insights that drive telecom operator success
The global standard for network and subscriber insights

Opensignal is the leading global provider of independent insights into consumers' connectivity experiences and choice of carrier.

Our proprietary solutions give mobile and broadband network operators the insights they need to profitably compete and win, from executive level scorecards and public validation to pin-point level engineering analytics and consumer decision dynamics.

Our collection methods are unique and transparent. They enable operators to continually improve connectivity for all. The company is headquartered in the United States, with offices in the UK, Canada, Continental Europe, and Asia.
