About Us :

The Backward Classes Welfare Department, a Department under the Govt. of West Bengal is working towards sustainable development of the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes of West Bengal. The main aim of the Department is to improve the quality of life of these socially and economically backward classes and enhancement of capability of the people belonging to these communities , so that they can be very much part & parcel of the mainstream of the Society.

Notice regarding new system of applying for OBC certificate *
×

All applicants from the classes in attached List have to apply from ‘APPLY FOR OBC’ option under ‘OBC’ menu. For previous certificate holders only having Digital Certificate No., has to put their previous Digital Certificate No. and certificate issue date in online application form. Manual OBC certificate holders may search for Digital Certificate No. from ‘CHECK CERTIFICATE’ option under ‘OBC’ menu. All other options for OBC certificate or application can also be found under ‘OBC’ menu.
In case of a change in the geographical area, old certificate holders have to apply afresh instead of opting for revalidation or reissue.
