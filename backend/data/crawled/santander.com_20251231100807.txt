Press Releases Group 04/12/2025 Santander named the best bank in Europe by The Banker magazine Santander has been named the ‘Best Bank in Europe’ by financial magazine The Banker, which praised the bank for its record financial performance, strong operational resilience and clear progress in its digital transformation across its major markets this year. The judges highlighted in particular the impact of Santander’s broad balanced geographic diversification, improved ratings, and growth strategy, strengthened by the acquisition of TSB in the UK and the Group’s strong performance in Spain, Portugal and Poland.
