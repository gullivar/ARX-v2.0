Source: Staatskanzlei Saarland/MN Pagination page 1 page 2 page 3 Pagination Topics Culture Economy Education Nature and the environment Science/ research Sport Tourism and vibrancy Work and families Video Of 2020.05.12 Saarland imagemovie Great things can grow from small beginnings – and this is now proven in film: To the tunes of Saarland-born composer Frank Nimsgern, a short film tells the story of how Saarland enables big things to grow from small beginnings. As illustrations serve individual success stories from global players, start-ups, scientists as well as regional art highlights and perfect recreational opportunities. Short personal portraits give witness to success stories ‘made in Saarland’. Activation required We would like to point out that data will be transmitted to YouTube following activation. Information about the purpose and scope of data collection, data processing by YouTube and your options to object can be found in the section "Social media and external content/YouTube videos" in our Datenschutzerklärung . I accept Beschreibung Private companies, civil administration bodies, organisations and the public are invited to make use of the unedited film: on their websites, while visiting clients or at trade shows – thus promoting Saarland as a great place to work and live. Pictures say more than words: special value can be added to job interviews to convince candidates of Saarland as a potential new place of residence.
