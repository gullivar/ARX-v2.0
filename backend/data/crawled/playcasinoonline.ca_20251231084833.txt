Free Casino Bonus – Important Conditions First let’s make sure that you know the basic terminology. Without this, it will be difficult for you to understand the material presented below. Bonus – additional credits provided to customers for the game. Deposit – replenishment of a personal account in a casino. Free spins – free rotations on slot machines. Wager – requirements for playing back bonus money. It is expressed by a coefficient, meaning how many times the total amount of the client’s bet should exceed the received credits. For example, you received a bonus of $100 and the wager is x30. You can withdraw the bonus after you make bets in the amount of $3000. Cashback – the return of a part of the lost money. Loyalty program – a multi-level system of customer rewards. Comp points – points that are awarded to players for bets. They can be changed for money, spent in a store or used in other ways.
