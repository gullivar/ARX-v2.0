About us

We collect, protect and provide access to millions of physical items and billions of digital records about Australia and Australians and will continue to do so into the future.

We work with libraries throughout Australia to give you access to library collections and services, and to Trove.

Visit us in Canberra or online and use our services, see an exhibition, or attend an event.
