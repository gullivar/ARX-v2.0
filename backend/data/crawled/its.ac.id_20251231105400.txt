SUKOLILO The ITS Sukolilo campus occupies an area of 180 hectares and is the main campus for teaching and learning activities. MANYAR The Manyar Campus is home to the Department of Civil Infrastructure Engineering and the Faculty of VOCATIONS. COKROAMINOTO The Cokroaminoto Campus is allocated for the Doctoral and Masters in Interdiciplinary School of Management Technology school, as well as several collaborative programs. BUNCITAN The Buncitan Campus is situated on Jl. Raya Buncitan No. 82, Dusun Buncitan, Buncitan, Kec.
