Frequently Asked Questions
What services does Sav.com offer?

Sav.com provides a comprehensive suite of services tailored for domain investors, businesses, and individual users. These include domain registration with access to a vast array of TLDs, domain management tools, and an intuitive user interface for updating DNS records and WHOIS details. Additionally, Sav.com offers a low-commission marketplace for buying and selling domains, free WHOIS privacy protection for most domains, and a powerful API designed for developers managing bulk domains or creating automated processes. With features like DNS services powered by Cloudflare, domain backordering, and website-building tools, Sav.com ensures a seamless online experience.

Why choose Sav.com as your domain registrar?

Sav.com is a preferred domain registrar for several reasons. It offers transparent pricing with no hidden fees, ensuring customers only pay what they expect. With a marketplace commission of just 4%, Sav.com stands out from competitors charging higher rates. Sav.com’s interface is designed for simplicity and ease of use, whether you’re registering a single domain or managing a large portfolio. Furthermore, the platform’s focus on security, with features like free WHOIS privacy and Cloudflare integration, guarantees peace of mind for users.

Does Sav.com offer discounts for bulk domain purchases?

Yes, Sav.com offers bulk pricing options for domain investors and businesses. Customers who register or transfer multiple domains can benefit from discounted rates, helping them scale their online presence cost-effectively. This makes Sav.com an excellent choice for individuals or organizations managing extensive domain portfolios.

Can I transfer my domains to Sav.com?

Transferring your domains to Sav.com is a straightforward process. By initiating a transfer from your current registrar, you can consolidate domain management under one account. Sav.com provides competitive renewal rates and ensures minimal downtime during the transfer process. Detailed step-by-step guidance is available on the platform to assist users through the process.

What security measures does Sav.com provide?

Sav.com takes domain security seriously. The platform offers free WHOIS privacy to protect your personal information, two-factor authentication (2FA) for account security, and DNS services powered by Cloudflare to shield against cyber threats like DDoS attacks. Additionally, Sav.com regularly updates its systems to comply with the latest security standards.

How can I get started with Sav.com?

Getting started with Sav.com is simple. Begin by creating a free account on the website. Once registered, you can search for available domains, transfer existing ones, or explore services like DNS management and website building. Sav.com’s user-friendly dashboard makes it easy to manage your online assets.
