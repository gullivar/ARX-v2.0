Check them out! DOAJ DOAJ and JCR JCR RACO has an automatic data exporting tool available that smoothes the inclusion to the Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ). Journals included in the Directory have an emblem available on the journal’s webpage and appear in the corresponding list. Journals included on the Journal Citation Reports (JCR) are also granted an emblem and will appear on the list with all the journals in RACO that are part of the JCR. RACO (Catalan Journals in Open Access) is a cooperative repository where can be consulted, in open access , the full-text from articles of scientific, cultural and scholar Catalan journals with the main purpose of increasing their visibility. For further information, you can check the inclusion criteria and the different modalities of participation .
