$ECX Token

$ECX is the heartbeat of our eco-powered movement - a green financial tool that rewards green works and fuels real-world impacts!

HIT Mechanism

Inspired by Bitcoin's Hardcoded Issuance Threshold (HIT) mechanism, ECOX follows a structured issuance model where token distribution gradually decreases.
