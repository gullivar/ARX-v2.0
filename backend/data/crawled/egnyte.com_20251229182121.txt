From collaboration to secure AI insights to content governance, Egnyte is the AI-powered content cloud built for the content and industry-specific workflows that power your business. Bringing all your content into a single shared repository, Egnyte helps to improve business outcomes by managing your most complex, mission-critical content, making smarter, data-driven decisions, and reducing risk.

Industry-Specific Solutions

Empower teams with specialized solutions for your mission-critical content in architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC), financial services, life sciences, and media and entertainment.

Accelerate project delivery with seamless collaboration on CAD, BIM, and PDFs across the office, field, and external partners, while leveraging AI insights and built-in governance.
