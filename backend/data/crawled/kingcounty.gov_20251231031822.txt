Free transit rides on New Year's Eve
Metro joins other regional transit agencies to provide free rides this New Year’s Eve, December 31. Riders can travel without paying fares on King County Metro, Sound Transit, Community Transit, Everett Transit, Pierce Transit and the Seattle Streetcar as part of their holiday celebrations.
