Inspire Innovation Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, a 501(c)(3) non-profit, powers transformational discoveries in Cancer, Genomics, Neuroscience, Artificial Intelligence, Plant Biology & Quantitative Biology by educating and empowering scientific pioneers. CSHL provides a powerful and productive environment to Discover, Develop, Connect & Share world-changing ideas that advance the field of bioscience — for more than 100 years, today and into the future. CSHL pursues the biggest questions in science. We cultivate and nurture a constant flow of ideas by inspiring Curiosity, Discovery & Innovation.
