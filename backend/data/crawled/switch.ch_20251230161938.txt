About us
EN
We are creating a secure digital future for us all.

We ensure that Switzerland is optimally networked as a location for research and higher education. And have been doing so since the early days of the internet. With broad expertise and many years of experience, our dedicated teams connect public and private institutions – securely and sustainably. As a leading driving force in various international bodies, we also ensure the seamless global integration of our university and research network.

Cloud Services

Our comprehensive cloud solutions combine operations and data services. This enables you to work efficiently and profitably – while always complying with the strictest Swiss security standards.

Procurement & Legal

Digital Identity

Our innovative technologies ensure a smart digital identity for all university members and other users that can be used across the board at all universities and beyond, never expires and supports lifelong learning. The Switch digital identity also ensures that your data remains in Switzerland and is subject to the strict Swiss data protection act.
Discover Digital identity

Research Network

We connect the university community with the right infrastructure – and we’ve been doing so since 1989. Switch LAN links universities with each other and with the internet. In addition to the data network, we also provide everything needed for optimal usage.
Discover Research network
