Universal WordPress Theme Support Universal WordPress Theme The Johns Hopkins Universal WordPress Theme (UWPT) is a simple and editable free resource for Johns Hopkins University affiliates. The theme was built by the IT@JH Design Team with guidance from university and medicine communication teams and follows branding guidelines. UWPT website owners have the ability to add and manage their own content, including navigation, header, pages, posts, forms, team member profiles, images, and PDFs.
