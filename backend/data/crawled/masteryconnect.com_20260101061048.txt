Mastery

Bridge the gap between assessment and everyday learning.

Mastery simplifies student assessments and helps you plan more productive interventions. And, of course, it connects seamlessly with Canvas to keep all your teaching and testing tools in one familiar place. 

Get a demo

How to Choose an Assessment Management System

Read the Guide

Assessments, content, and insights—all at your fingertips

One connected ecosystem
High-quality content
Comprehensive reporting
Connected teams

Streamline educational journeys

One integrated learning ecosystem lets students and educators seamlessly navigate between course content in Canvas and assessments in Mastery—all in a familiar environment with a single sign-on. 

Build, share, and track assessments all in one place.
Save time with automated grading, reporting, and analytics.
Track student progress toward proficiency across assignments and assessments.

Reduce legwork with pre-built content

Flexible assessments and a massive library of pre-built, research-validated content make it easy to create everything from rigorous exams to quick follow-up formative assessments. 

Access research-validated, standards-aligned assessment content.
Measure student performance quickly and accurately.
Target individual needs with specific, timely feedback.

Get the digestible data and insights you need

Automated scoring and intuitive reports help you quickly understand student progress and plan purposeful interventions. Canvas data synchronization enables a holistic view of student performance across assignments and assessments. 

View data-based insights by student, classroom, or the entire district.
Fuel productive conversations with students and families.
Predict end-of-year assessment outcomes (not available in all states—chat with us for more info).

Encourage alignment and collaboration

Create a culture of collaboration with shared resources across teacher teams, and implement common assessment practices to ensure a standards-aligned learning environment.

Deliver consistent assessment resources across classrooms.
Reduce admin time with a shared library of pre-vetted content.
Encourage data-driven conversations about student growth.

Less mystery. More mastery.
