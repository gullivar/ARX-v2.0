Frequently asked questions about Moneris
What types of payment solutions does Moneris offer?
Moneris has a wide range of end-to-end payment solutions, from in-store terminals, self-serve kiosks and mobile POS solutions to online checkout integrations and an all-in-one ecommerce platform. Whether you're running a café, clinic or clothing store, we’ve got the tools to help you accept payments your way!  
Is Moneris right for small businesses?
Absolutely. We support businesses of all sizes across Canada, from small side hustles to nationwide enterprises. Our commerce solutions are scalable, which means whether you’re just starting out or growing fast, we can tailor a setup that works for you. 
How secure are Moneris transactions?
Very. We lead the industry in payment security, with advanced fraud prevention tools and PCI-compliant systems that keep your transactions and your customers safe in real time before there’s an impact on your business. 
Can Moneris help my business sell both in-store and online?
Yes! We specialize in omnichannel commerce. Our integrated systems make it easy to manage payments across physical locations and ecommerce platforms, all while giving you a comprehensive view of inventory, sales, analytics and more. 
What kind of customer support does Moneris provide?
We’re here for you 24/7. Whether you have a quick question or need in-depth guidance, our bilingual support team is ready to help with real answers from real people. 
Why should I choose Moneris over other payment providers?
We’re a proudly Canadian business, backed by two Canadian banks (BMO and RBC) and focused on powering commerce right here at home. With 25 years and counting of fintech expertise, trusted security and tailored commerce solutions, Moneris is the partner merchants from coast to coast to coast rely on to start, run and grow their passion.
