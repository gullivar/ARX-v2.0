What's my IP?

A simple web service to show you your current IP address supporting both IPv4 and IPv6.

Ever needed to get your public IP address programmatically? Maybe you're setting up new cloud services and need to know your IP, maybe you're behind a firewall and need to tunnel information. Whatever the reason, sometimes knowing your public IP address can be useful.
