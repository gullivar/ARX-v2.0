We’ve got answers. What is Toniebox 2? Chevron up Chevron down Toniebox 2 is an audio player that uses Tonie audio characters to play songs and stories and Tonieplay Games to unlock interactive stories, quizzes, challenges and more. Will our Tonies still work on Toniebox 2? Chevron up Chevron down They will! All their favourite Tonies will still work on Toniebox 2 the same way they work on Toniebox 1. Will new tonies® products work on Toniebox 1? Chevron up Chevron down All Tonies will work on Toniebox 1 and Toniebox 2, but Tonieplay Games will only work on Toniebox 2. What is Tonieplay? Chevron up Chevron down Designed for Toniebox 2, Tonieplay turns the Toniebox into a safe and screen-free gaming experience. You’ll need a Tonieplay Controller to play any of the Tonieplay Games – both are sold separately. Do I need WiFi to use the Toniebox 2? Chevron up Chevron down The Toniebox will need to be connected to WiFi for set up and the first time you play a Tonie or a Tonieplay Game. Once the content has downloaded then you’re good to go and don’t need to be connected to play. What age is the Toniebox 2 for? Chevron up Chevron down The Toniebox 2 is certified safe for 1+ and beyond. There are Tonies and Tonieplay Games to keep everyone entertained for years and years.
