Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology
Helping dermatologists improve patient outcomes

The Official Publication of the American Academy of Dermatology The Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology (JAAD), the official scientific publication of the American Academy of Dermatology (AAD), aims to satisfy the educational needs of the dermatology community. More

Society Information

The American Academy of Dermatology Opens in new window   was founded in 1938. It is the largest, most influential and representative dermatology group in the United States. With a membership of more than 19,000, it represents virtually all practicing dermatologists in the United States, as well as a growing number of international dermatologists. More Opens in new window  

 

CME Supplement

Hidradenitis suppurativa: Embracing progress

JAAD Case Reports Call for Submissions on GLP-1 Agonists

JAAD Case Reports announces a new Virtual Special Issue (VSI) focusing on the association of GLP-1 agonists with cutaneous disease. Areas of focus include novel uses of GLP-1 agonists in dermatology, direct and indirect adverse effects of GLP-1 agonist usage, and procedural treatments for potential dermatologic manifestations from the use of these agents.
