Embark on your adventure at U of I
Explore what’s possible.

Find your passion from among more than 200 areas of study. University of Idaho’s statewide footprint sets you on a journey of discovery. At Idaho’s only Carnegie R1 classified research university, you’ll get hands-on experiences that will shape your future. Nestled in the supportive community of Moscow, the ivy-covered campus provides the perfect setting for you to thrive academically and connect with other Vandals who will shape the future.
