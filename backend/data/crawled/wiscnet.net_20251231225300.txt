We connect people and strategies to solve common technology challenges and to develop innovative solutions in support of educational, research, and community service missions. What is WiscNet? What are the benefits of being a WiscNet member? WiscNet is more than a network provider — it’s a trusted community that connects people and strategies through listening, learning, and thoughtful innovation, backed by expert support, shared services, and opportunities to connect and grow. Membership means a stable, mission-driven partnership with people who understand your realities and your needs, turn what we hear into practical action, and stand alongside you — so you can focus on what matters most to your organization and the people you serve. There are a lot of practical benefits too! Visit our WiscNet Membership page to learn much more. Contact Support WiscNet Services WiscNet, with 100-gigabit interfaces in Chicago and Minneapolis, procures commodity Internet from top-tier providers. We aim for on-net traffic to enhance user experience and lower costs via peering with educational, research, and commercial networks. WiscNet's E-rate eligible Network Access Service offers unlimited, unmeasured internet at a fixed annual fee, even when members boost their capacity, ensuring optimal utilization without any extra charges. WiscNet Events WiscNet Governance WiscNet, a non-profit association, is committed to fostering education, research, and economic development in Wisconsin through a digital network interconnecting public and private entities. Its mission includes promoting collaboration, facilitating access to regional and national networks, and enhancing scientific knowledge.
