What is a home warranty? A home warranty is an annual, renewable service contract that provides peace of mind and protection. When a covered home system or appliance breaks down, your home warranty plan covers the repair costs. It complements your homeowners insurance by protecting things your insurance doesn’t. In addition to helping with the cost and hassle to repair or replace parts of covered home systems and appliance that break down over time due to normal wear and tear, our home warranty plans also offer American Home Shield members exclusive pricing on home maintenance services, new appliances and HVAC systems, and more – from tech and smart home installation services to pre-season HVAC tune-ups , and more Why do I need a home warranty? A home warranty is a smart way to avoid unexpected and expensive home system and appliance repair costs. When a covered item breaks down due to normal wear and tear, we’ll help cover the repair cost and you have the added convenience of only one company to contact. No more searching for the right home repair professional . We’ll assign a qualified Pro from our network to your service request who will get the job done right. With American Home Shield, you don’t have to face home repairs alone. You can leave the stress to us. What’s covered by a home warranty? We offer three different home warranty plan options so you can choose the one that’s best for you and your home. Our entry level plan, Shield Silver covers the parts and components of your heating and AC, plumbing , and electrical systems . Our ShieldGold plan covers your systems and hardworking kitchen and laundry appliances like your dishwasher , refrigerator , washer and dryer, and more. And finally, our ShieldPlatinum plan covers your systems and appliances, but also includes roof leak repair coverage, unlimited AC refrigerant, and more member perks. With American Home Shield, it’s easy to cover the items that keep your home running smoothly and choose a plan that’s right for you. How much does a home warranty cost? Every home is unique, so it’s only fitting that every home warranty plan is too. We use three key factors to determine the cost of your plan . Size and location of your home – Depending on where you live, your cost could include state or local taxes. We use the details that you provide when you request a quote to inform you of your full plan costs upfront. Plan type – The home warranty plan you select factors into the cost. We offer three plan options, so you simply need to choose the plan that best meets your home’s needs. Service Fee – You will pay a Service Fee each time you place a service request. It’s the standard cost that covers the time your repair Pro spends assessing the issue and figuring out how to fix it. You get to choose your Service Fee amount when your purchase your home warranty plan. It's easy to get a free home warranty quote in minutes by entering your zip code at the top of this page.
