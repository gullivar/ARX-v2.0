FAQs

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What types of memory and processors do your servers use, and at what frequencies?

On virtual hosting processor 2 x Intel Xeon E5-2630v3 RAM 256 GB DDR4 ECC. Virtual private servers use Intel Xeon processors of different models from the latest v3-v5 lines with a core frequency of 2400 MHz. RAM is mostly used - DDR-3. HP Proliant G9 dedicated servers have DDR-4 and processors: Intel Xeon Silver 4116, 2xIntel E5-2430v2, Intel E5-2650v2, 2xAMD EPYC 7402, 2xAMD EPYC 7702, 2xIntel E5-2630v4, Intel Xeon Gold 6134, 2xIntel Xeon Gold 6134. For cloud VPS - HP Generation G10 servers with powerful Intel Xeon Gold 6148 processors. Core clock frequency 2.4-3.70 GHz, 20 cores, 40 threads.

When will the service or server be issued?

The service will be issued within a few seconds to 30 minutes. When you rent a dedicated server, it will be issued within a few hours to 5 business days, depending on equipment availability. Depending on the OS, the cloud VPS activation process usually takes a few minutes. Activation time can vary depending on your chosen operating system and any customizations you request. Linux deploys in 1 minute. Windows deploys in 2 minutes. Installing the OS from your image may take longer.

What payment methods do you accept?

At the moment, the following payment methods are available: -Stripe: Payment by bank cards, including VISA, MasterCard, and American Express. -PayPal -Coinify: We accept BTS cryptocurrency and alternative cryptocurrencies. -Methods of payment with us may vary from country to country. If you haven’t found a convenient way to pay, write to us in chat, and we will advise you further.

How can I determine the best hosting plan?

To choose a suitable hosting tariff, virtual server rental, or dedicated server, you need to analyze the needs of your project and future plans. Determine yourself or check with developers what resources (CPU, RAM, disk space, bandwidth) are required. Take into account current needs and possible future growth. If you are in doubt, we can advise you in our online chat or in a ticket.

What control panels do you use?

CPanel is preinstalled on virtual hosting. You can install any desired OS or server control panel on virtual servers. We support the following panels: VestaCP, ISPManager, and CPanel.

How do I receive a refund for a service if I no longer need it?

If you no longer require the service, please contact our customer service department, and we will return the balance of unused funds to your account.

How quickly can I receive a response from your technical support team?

Our technical support team works 24/7, 365 days a year. Response times to a request range from 5 to 30 minutes.

How can I upgrade my Shared Hosting and/or virtual server services?

If you have purchased a shared hosting service but are in the process of development or improvements and see that you need more than the resources, you just need to choose a higher tariff in your personal cabinet. Don’t worry; the tariff will expand while preserving all data and recalculating the paid period. If you need another tariff for a VPS server or dedicated server, you can migrate to a more productive server or add RAM/memory by contacting our tech support.

What are your NS servers?

Our NSs: ns1.zomro.net ns2.zomro.ru ns3.zomro.com ns4.zomro.su

Do you offer unlimited traffic, and what is the channel's bandwidth?

Traffic on shared hosting and virtual servers is unlimited. Channel bandwidth on virtual servers from 250 to 500 Mbit/s. With the exception of dedicated servers, where the bandwidth channel is from 100Mbps to 10Gbps. The price of each cloud server includes a paid amount of Internet traffic. There are no traffic restrictions, and additional funds will be charged only for traffic overruns. Channel bandwidth on cloud servers is 1000Mbps.

Do you have free SSL?

Yes. You can install the free SSL, Let's Encrypt. If you have any difficulties, you can always contact our technical support.

How reliable is your network and equipment?

Our network is of premium quality because we use only the most modern network equipment. It protects against DDoS attacks. In addition, there is a vast network capacity with all major TIER-I transit providers. Our servers are located in TIER-III level data centers.
