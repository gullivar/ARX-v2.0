First Fridays January 2 Art in Tune January 15 MLK Day January 19 See All Events Our Gift to You: More Time with Homer The response to “Of Light and Air: Winslow Homer in Watercolor” has been incredible—so we’re giving you even more opportunities to see it by extending weekend hours! Every Saturday and Sunday beginning December 27, you can visit the exhibition until 6 pm. For a limited time in New American Cafe we're serving a soft pretzel and beer cheese appetizer paired with Maine Beer Company’s Lunch IPA, in honor of Homer’s home and studio in Prouts Neck, Maine, and keeping the popular exhibition shop open late . Don’t miss the final Saturday, January 17, when we’re open until 8 pm for one last evening with Homer! Gift of Membership Celebrate creativity, support the arts, and spread cheer this holiday season.
