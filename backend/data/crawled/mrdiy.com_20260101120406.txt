MR D.I.Y. is Malaysia's leading home improvement retailer, with over 1,400 stores nationwide across three brands—MR D.I.Y., MR.DOLLAR, and MR.TOY—and in Brunei. It is also the master franchisee of the EMTOP brand in Malaysia. MR D.I.Y. offers a wide range of approximately 17,000 products in five main categories: hardware, household and furnishing, electrical, stationery and sports equipment, and others. MR D.I.Y. focuses on putting customers first by offering a variety of quality products at affordable prices, staying true to its motto: ALWAYS LOW PRICES.
