Update Your Mobile Number

Driving license holders and registered Vehicle owners are urged to ensure that their mobile number is updated in the Vahan and Sarathi portal. This will help them in ensuring that the details are complete, accurate, and up to date. Online facility has been provided to update mobile number in the portal without visiting RTOs.

Scan Or Click To Update Your Mobile Number-

Vahan
https://vahan.parivahan.gov.in/mobileupdate/
Sarathi
https://sarathi.parivahan.gov.in/sarathiservice/mobNumUpdpub.do
