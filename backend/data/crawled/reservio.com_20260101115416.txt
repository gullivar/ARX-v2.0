Reservio is an all-in-one booking and scheduling software designed for service-based small businesses such as hair salons , barbershops , yoga studios , or wellness centers . It lets clients conveniently book appointments , classes , or events online anytime, anywhere—no phone calls or emails needed. For businesses, Reservio offers a clear scheduling calendar , automated reminders , client and staff management tools, plus integrated payment processing and POS system . Beyond reservations, it provides everything you need to stay organized, streamline communication, and deliver a seamless booking experience that saves time and boosts customer satisfaction . How does the online booking system work? A booking system lets clients see your availability, choose a time, and confirm an appointment , class , or event instantly. Businesses manage everything in a digital scheduling calendar , while clients enjoy 24/7 booking convenience. With Reservio , you get a free booking website where clients can: Explore your services and pricing Check staff availability Schedule appointments or classes Pay securely online You can also share a booking link or QR code on social media, email, or printed materials. On the business side, all bookings are managed via the web platform or the Reservio Business mobile app , giving you full control of schedules, staff , and payments on the go. This makes Reservio not just free software but one of the most complete appointment scheduling systems for small businesses . What is the difference between appointment scheduling and online booking? Appointment scheduling software is mainly used by service providers to manage their calendars , organize appointments, and automate reminders . For businesses with multiple staff members, it can also help coordinate employee schedules and keep the whole team in sync. An online booking system , on the other hand, is the client-facing tool that lets customers book appointments, classes, or events online 24/7. With Reservio , you get both in one platform: powerful appointment scheduling software for managing your business and a seamless online booking system that makes reservations simple for your clients. Plus, Reservio includes payment processing , client management features, a built-in POS system , and more —making it an all-in-one business management solution for growing your business. Does Reservio send automated reminders to clients? Yes. Reservio includes automated messaging features that let you send reminders via SMS or email before each booking. These notifications help reduce no-shows and ensure clients never miss their appointments . You can customize messages, choose when they’re sent, and use them to improve client satisfaction. For service-based businesses, such as beauty professionals , barbershops , gyms , and more, automated reminders are one of the most effective tools in any online scheduling software, helping to cut missed bookings and keep clients coming back. Is it possible to accept payments from customers with Reservio? Absolutely. Reservio integrates an online reservation system with a built-in point-of-sale (POS) system . This means you can: Accept secure payments online during booking Process in-person transactions Track all sales in one place When clients book through your website , booking link , or QR code, they can pay immediately—helping you secure revenue upfront and reduce cancellations.
