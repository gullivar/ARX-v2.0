Ad comment (16) Bye-Bye, 2025 Posted by Random Talking Bush - December 28, 2025 12:56 AM As the year comes to a close (the less said about this year's events, the better I feel), it's time for one final batch of sprites from our community (including a hefty amount of customs!) to end off this year. With a suprising amount of variety, it's hard to give a shout-out to anything in particular (well, other than the efforts to keep the Fire Emblem Heroes, Idolm@ster, One Piece and Osomatsu mobile game pages up-to-date, which is always appreciated since they always add content so frequently!) so I'll just keep it brief and let the submissions speak for themselves. We've got the usual mainstays of Mario, Kirby, Undertale/Deltarune and Sonic (including those from a certain prototype unearthed recently) submissions, as well as plenty of Brok: The InvestiGator (another game I've been meaning to play), Fate/Hollow Ataraxia, Hamtaro, JoJo, Toushin Toshi, Umineko, Z.A.T.O., and many, many more! Check 'em all out, and in the meantime, have yourself a merry well-after-Christmas-by-now, and I'll see y'all again in 2026!
