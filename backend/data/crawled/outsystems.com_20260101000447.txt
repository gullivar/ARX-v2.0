OutSystems is an AI-powered low-code platform that empowers every team to turn ideas into AI-powered applications and agents that drive revenue, efficiency, and customer impact. With the help of visual development, pre-built reusable building blocks, and AI assistance throughout the SDLC, OutSystems makes it possible to build, manage, and scale mission-critical solutions 10x faster than standard code—without compromising on security, governance, or performance. What is OutSystems used for? You can build anything with OutSystems, but most OutSystems customers focus on building enterprise-ready apps and agents that drive real business results. Thanks to the power of low-code and AI, you can create perfect-fit, custom applications and agents with the efficiency of off-the-shelf software—without the risk of costly lock-in. To learn more about what OutSystems customers are building and the results they’re seeing, check out our customer stories . Is OutSystems an AI low-code platform? OutSystems is an AI-powered low-code platform, which means that it combines a low-code foundation with powerful AI capabilities . In addition to visual development and pre-built reusable building blocks, OutSystems gives you all the AI power you need to accelerate time-to-market and orchestrate human and AI collaboration. OutSystems Mentor is your AI partner for transforming app generation, delivery, and monitoring. With an AI-driven development experience and a unique agentic AI architecture, Mentor streamlines the entire SDLC, helping you build faster and smarter. OutSystems Agent Workbench enables you to create custom agents that deliver real results. Develop and orchestrate AI agents for any use case—across any department, workflow, or data—to streamline operations, elevate experiences, and grow revenue. Is OutSystems low-code fit for large enterprises? Yes. In fact, OutSystems is purpose-built for large enterprises, combining the speed and agility of low-code with the scalability, security, and governance that global organizations demand. Enterprises trust OutSystems to build and modernize mission-critical applications because it can handle complex integrations, high-performance workloads, and enterprise-grade security requirements, all while ensuring compliance with global standards. With robust DevOps, lifecycle management, and cloud deployment options, OutSystems empowers IT teams to deliver innovation at scale—without sacrificing reliability or control. Is OutSystems an AI app generator platform? OutSystems is much more than an app generator platform! While our AI-powered low-code platform does offer powerful app generation capabilities that help teams quickly create applications, OutSystems goes far beyond that by enabling enterprises to build, integrate, and scale mission-critical systems with full lifecycle management, enterprise-grade security, and governance. Unlike simple generators that stop at the first version of an app, OutSystems provides the flexibility and depth to customize, extend, and continuously evolve applications—ensuring they meet the complex and ever-changing needs of large organizations. Can I use OutSystems for custom AI agent development? Yes—OutSystems enables custom AI agent development through Agent Workbench . With Agent Workbench, teams can create custom AI agents that are deeply integrated into their business processes and applications, and can be used to streamline operations, elevate experiences, and grow revenue. Unlike standalone AI tools, OutSystems provides the governance, security, and scalability required for enterprise use, while giving teams the flexibility to create agents tailored to their unique needs. This means organizations can innovate quickly with AI while maintaining the reliability and control they expect from OutSystems.
