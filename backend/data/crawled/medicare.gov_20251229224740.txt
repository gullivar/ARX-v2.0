In a Medicare Advantage Plan? You can still make a change!

From January 1 – March 31, if you’re in a Medicare Advantage Plan, you can switch plans or return to Original Medicare and join a separate Medicare drug plan. You can also add your providers while you’re comparing plans and find out if they participate in the plans you’re considering.
