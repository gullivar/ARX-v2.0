Our C-Suite and leaders are all simply amazed.” – Director of talent acquisition Read more FAQ What is iCIMS? iCIMS is a comprehensive enterprise-grade applicant tracking system and AI-powered recruitment software designed to help talent acquisition teams and hiring managers streamline the recruiting process, with tools for candidate relationship management, employer branding, onboarding, and more. How does iCIMS improve the hiring process? iCIMS helps you hire faster and smarter. AI and automation speed up resume screening, candidate matching, interview scheduling, and communication. This reduces repetitive tasks so your team can focus on connecting with the right people and building stronger relationships. Can iCIMS integrate with other platforms and job boards? Yes. iCIMS connects with more than 800 partner technologies and supports thousands of integrations. That includes job boards, background checks, HR and payroll systems, assessment tools, and social media channels. With flexible APIs and prebuilt connectors, you can simplify your tech stack and keep data flowing seamlessly. Does iCIMS support high volume hiring? Absolutely. iCIMS is built to handle large-scale hiring needs across industries like retail, hospitality, and healthcare. The platform supports bulk candidate management, automated communications, self-service interview scheduling, and conversational application flows, making it easier to engage thousands of candidates quickly while maintaining a great experience. How does iCIMS enhance candidate engagement? iCIMS helps you connect with candidates on their terms. Mobile-friendly applications, SMS and email campaigns, branded career sites, and AI-powered chat assistants keep job seekers informed and engaged. These tools build trust and strengthen relationships throughout the hiring journey. How does iCIMS protect candidate and company data? Security and compliance are at the core of the iCIMS platform. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with role-based access and detailed audit trails. iCIMS is also certified under TrustArc’s Responsible AI framework and supports global compliance standards like GDPR and CCPA. Does iCIMS support global and multilingual recruiting? Yes. Organizations in more than 200 countries rely on iCIMS to support hiring worldwide. The platform offers multi-language career sites, localized candidate workflows, and compliance tools to meet global hiring requirements. What kind of training and support does iCIMS provide? iCIMS offers a full support ecosystem that includes implementation services, live and on-demand training, certification programs, a self-service knowledge base, and a dedicated customer success team. You’ll also have access to community forums, developer resources, and ongoing product education.
