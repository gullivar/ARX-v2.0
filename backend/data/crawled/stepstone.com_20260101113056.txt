We believe in the right job for everyone We’re working towards a world where every person has a role that perfectly aligns with their unique skills and aspirations. A world where workers are happy, organizations are productive, and societies are prosperous. Technological innovation is in our DNA We’ve been revolutionizing how companies and jobseekers connect via tech, right from the moment we founded one of the world’s first job boards in 1995. Today, our unique AI-powered products are fundamental to how companies and talents match globally. How our business works Our industry-leading job ad technology is the cornerstone of our two primary business models. Our advanced AI-powered job marketplaces provide employers with unparalleled access to rich, diverse talent pools, leveraging cutting-edge proprietary matching technology to deliver great hires. Our innovative programmatic-powered recruitment marketing solutions empower employers to attract both active and passive candidates through a comprehensive array of channels, maximizing reach and engagement.
