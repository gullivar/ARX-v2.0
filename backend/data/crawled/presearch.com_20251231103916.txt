Mobile Browsers Desktop Browser Extensions Frequently Asked Questions What makes Presearch different? Presearch is a decentralized search engine that prioritizes privacy and gives you unbiased results. Unlike traditional search engines, we don't track your searches or build a profile about you. How does privacy protection work? We use advanced encryption and a decentralized network of nodes to protect your privacy. Your searches are distributed across the network, and no single entity has access to your complete search history. Can I earn rewards? Yes! You can earn PRE tokens for your searches. These tokens can be used to stake nodes, advertise on the platform, or traded on exchanges. The more you use Presearch, the more you earn. What are nodes? Nodes are community-operated servers that power the Presearch network. Node operators stake PRE tokens and earn rewards for processing searches and maintaining network integrity.
