What Types of Proxies Does NetNut Offer?

NetNut provides a comprehensive suite of proxy solutions tailored to diverse business needs:

Residential Proxies: Route your traffic through real residential IPs for high anonymity and low block rates. Ideal for web scraping, ad verification, and accessing geo-restricted content with over 85 million IPs worldwide.

Rotating Residential Proxies: Access over 85 million IPs across 195+ countries, ideal for large-scale data collection and maintaining anonymity.

Static Residential Proxies: Utilize 1 million+ always-on IPs for consistent sessions, perfect for account management and long-term scraping tasks.

Mobile Proxies: Leverage 3G/4G/5G/LTE connections from real mobile carriers, ensuring high success rates and minimal detection.

Datacenter Proxies: Benefit from 150,000+ high-speed IPs with 99.99% uptime, suitable for performance-intensive operations.

Each proxy type is designed to offer optimal performance, reliability, and scalability for various use cases.
