ACADEMIC SPOTLIGHT

Music

From the concert hall to the recording studio, WIU’s Music program empowers students to perform, compose, and inspire through the universal language of sound. Here are some of the highlights:

Dozens of scholarship opportunities
Multiple auditioned and non-auditioned ensembles open to all students
Visiting performers from around the world each year
A brand-new Performing Arts Center opening in early 2026
150+ performances year-round
National performance opportunities (Carnegie Hall in May 2025, Marching Band in Hawaii & more)
Applied faculty in every instrument and voice type
Esteemed faculty with world-class training and industry experience
