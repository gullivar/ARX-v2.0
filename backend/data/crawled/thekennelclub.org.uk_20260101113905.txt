Join Our Pack For centuries, people have spoken of ‘owner and pet’. But the truth is, that story has always been the wrong way round. Our new Royal prefix doesn’t change who we are, it simply renews our energy and our sense of duty to celebrate the truth: It’s dogs who bring joy, enrich our days, and ultimately rule our lives. They are the ones that we would do anything for. From everyday health to lifelong protection, from wagging tails to winning rosettes, the Royal Kennel Club is here to honour dogs with the care, distinction and happiness they deserve.
