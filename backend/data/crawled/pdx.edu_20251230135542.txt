COMPUTERWORLD NAMES PSU A 2026 BEST PLACE TO WORK IN IT

Portland State University has again been named a Computerworld Best Place to Work in IT for 2026. This marks the ninth consecutive year PSU’s Office of Information Technology has earned Top 100 recognition for its supportive culture, innovation and commitment to staff growth.

STUDY WARNS OF HEALTH AND FINANCIAL RISKS FROM DELAYED HEP B VACCINE

New research from the OHSU-PSU School of Public Health shows that even brief delays to the infant Hepatitis B birth dose could lead to thousands of preventable infections, hundreds of additional cancer cases and deaths, and more than $200M in excess healthcare costs each year.
