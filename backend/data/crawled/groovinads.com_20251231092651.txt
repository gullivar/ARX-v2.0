The opt-out process does not imply that you won’t see any more advertising served by Groovinads,but only that we won’t store any data from your interaction with our ads. The opt-out process works setting a cookie with the value opt-out in your browser and eliminating previous Groovinads cookies. So every time you browse sites with advertising served by Groovinads your browser will send the OPTOUT value to Groovinads, indicating that you don’t want our cookies in your browser. If you delete the cookies from your browser, you will have to opt-out again. If you use multiple browsers on your computer, the process should be done once for each browser. It is also necessary that your browser accepts cookies from third parties to make the process work. GROOVINADS CANNOT AND DOES NOT USE COOKIES TO DETERMINE ANY INDIVIDUAL’S ACTUAL IDENTITY .
