Who we are

Mattel163 is a leading game studio creating highly engaging mobile games for a global audience. Founded in 2017 as a joint venture between the iconic toy company Mattel and the Chinese internet giant NetEase, Mattel163 employs nearly 300 talented people in offices across Los Angeles and Hangzhou. With more than 450 million players to date, Mattel163 is the name behind some of the highest-grossing and most engaging mobile games developed based on iconic toy IPs from Mattel as well as its own original IPs. Join us in connecting the world through play!
