The information found in the guidelines is for healthcare providers and consumers alike. Why PrEP? PrEP, or pre-exposure prophylaxis, is an effective medication that prevents HIV. When taken as prescribed, it lowers the risk of getting HIV by about 99% through sex and by at least 74% for people who inject druges. Find out if PrEP is right for you. Aging and HIV Today, thanks to improvements in the effectiveness of treatment with HIV medicine (called antiretroviral therapy or ART), people with HIV who are diagnosed early and who get and stay on ART can keep the virus suppressed and live long and healthy lives.
