National Day Offer FAQ’s 1 GIGA 1. Who is eligible for the 1 Gbps National Day offer? Both existing and new customers. 2. How much does the 1 Gbps upgrade cost? The promotional price is 100 Baisa per month, valid for 12 months. 3. Is the offer available on all plans? No. It is available only on: Fibernet Home 28, Fibernet Home 30, Fibernet Home 35 with a 24-month contract for both new and existing customers. 4. What happens after the 12-month promotional period? The 1 Gbps add-on expires, and the customer returns to the standard speed included in their base plan. 6. Does the base plan price change? No. The 100 Baisa add-on charge will stop after 12 months. The base plan price remains unchanged. 7. Can customers cancel the add-on before the 12 months are over? Yes. It can be removed before the 12-month period ends, but if removed it can no longer be added and the speed will return to the normal base plan speeds. 8. Can existing customers who are not on 28/30/35 become eligible? Yes. They can upgrade their plan to Fibernet 28/30/35 (24-month contract) and apply the offer. 9. What happens if the customer cancels the service before the contract ends? Early termination fees will apply, as the 1 Gbps offer is linked to a 24-month commitment. 10. Is the 1 Gbps upgrade permanent? No. It lasts for 12 months only as part of the National Day promotion. 10 GIGA 1. What is the 10 GIGA Limited Offer? This is a special National Day promotion offering the 10 Giga plan at a reduced price of OMR 281 per month, instead of the standard rate of OMR 500. 2. Who is eligible for the 1 Gbps National Day offer? Both existing and new customers. 3. What contract durations qualify for this offer? You can avail the discounted price with either a 12-month or 24-month contract. 4. How long will the discounted price remain active? The special rate of OMR 281/month is valid throughout your selected contract period (12 or 24 months). 5. What happens when my contract ends? After the contract expires, the price automatically reverts to the standard rate of OMR 500 per month. 6. Is this a limited-time promotion? Yes. This offer is part of a limited-time National Day offer. 7. Does the offer apply automatically? No. You will need to subscribe or renew your contract to activate the special pricing.
