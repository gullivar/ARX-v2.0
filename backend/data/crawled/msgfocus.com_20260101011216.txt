Why am I here? Why am I here? We are Adestra . We provide email newsletter and marketing services to a variety of
			clients around the world. This site is used to host images, pages
			and other services used in emails that our clients send using our
			platform. Normally, email recipients shouldn't see this page, but there are
			several reasons why you might have ended up here: Broken link in email If you have come here from a link in an email, that link may be
			broken. Please try copying the link out of the original email and
			pasting into your browser's address bar. Unsubscribe from a mailing list If you are attempting to unsubscribe from a mailing sent by one of
			our clients using our system, then the quickest method is clicking
			the 'unsubscribe' link in the email you were sent; they should all
			contain one, and they do work. Received an email in error If you feel you have been sent an email in error, or you have
			experienced problems unsubscribing, please contact our Abuse team
			at abuse@adestra.com . They
			will investigate your issue and ensure it is addressed. Questions, complaints or any other concerns If you have any questions or comments regarding our services, you
			can contact us via the contact page on our website . If you wish to make a complaint, or have any questions or comments
			regarding a specific email we have sent, please feel free to contact
			our Abuse team at abuse@adestra.com .
			It may help us if you can forward or attach a copy of the email that
			you have received.
