About Our Studio
Founded in June 2024 in Singapore, Nebula is a mobile game studio on a mission to redefine the way people experience gaming. Our vision is simple yet bold: to craft engaging and innovative mobile games.
From casual puzzle games to competitive multiplayer experiences, our games are designed to entertain, challenge, and inspire players of all ages. Fueled by a passionate team of developers and designers, Nebula is committed to bringing the magic of gaming to every corner of the world.
