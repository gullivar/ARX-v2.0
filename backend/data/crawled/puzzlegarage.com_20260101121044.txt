About the game Online puzzles is a logic game for adults and children that can be played on a computer, tablet or smartphone. Multiplayer Puzzles is an online version of the game for several players. Regular Jigsaw Puzzles playing improves memory, mental clarity and slow the aging of brain cells. Rules of the game At the beginning of the game, the selected picture is divided into many small fragments of various shapes, the number of which depends on the chosen difficulty. The higher the difficulty, the more fragments you need to collect. The goal of the game is to return the fragments to their places in order to restore the original image in the shortest possible time.
