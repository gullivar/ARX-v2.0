ABOUT
US

Crazy Maple Studio was founded in San Francisco, USA in 2017, with offices in multiple locations, including Silicon Valley and Los Angeles in the US, Canada, Mexico, the Philippines, and Beijing and Shenzhen in China. We are a globally leading Internet content creation and distribution company. By focusing on content expansion, we have built a diversified product matrix that includes short dramas, interactive games, and novel-reading platform, and have created a vibrant creator ecosystem. CMS has been named by Time Magazine as one of the "2024 TIME100 Most Influential Companies".

A Vibrant Team of Innovators
At the heart of our success is a powerful team of creators passionate about pushing the boundaries of storytelling. Our diverse group brings unique perspectives and innovative ideas to every project, crafting content that resonates with audiences worldwide. United by a common goal, we strive to redefine entertainment and deliver experiences that are as impactful as they are engaging.
A Diverse Library of Entertainment
Explore a diverse range of genres across all our apps. Immerse yourself in our vast library, featuring a continually growing collection of captivating dramas. Whether you're drawn to steamy romances with billionaires, heart-wrenching tragedies, epic comebacks, or thrilling tales of love and revenge, there’s always an app perfectly suited to your tastes!
Connecting with Millions Worldwide
We are proud to connect millions of users around the world. By embracing diverse perspectives, backgrounds, and cultures, we enhance our content and resonate with a global audience.
Industry Trailblazers
Our primary goal is to entertain and engage global audiences. We aspire to inspire, connect, and bring joy through compelling storytelling and immersive experiences.
