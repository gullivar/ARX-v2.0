Our aim is to provide great homes and create vibrant communities where people can prosper.  

We're one of the UK’s oldest not-for-profit housing associations. And we want to provide the best possible experience for you, our Peabody residents. So we’re listening and working hard to improve things that aren’t quite right. And making sure everything we do has a positive impact on our homes, communities and the future.
