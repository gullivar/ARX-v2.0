Quick reminders for PCSO lotto winners

If you win, the lotto prize ticket has one (1) year validity. Unclaimed prizes within one (1) year will be forfeited. Likewise, the player is solely responsible for checking the accuracy of the data printed on the ticket(s), including bet type, bet amount, the draw date you want to enter, and the numbers you want to play.

Interested players below eighteen (18) years old cannot play any PCSO games.

Also, don’t buy your lotto ticket from individuals claiming the numbers and tickets will win. You can get it only from authorized PCSO outlets.

Do not subject the ticket to high temperature. Remember that the PCSO-generated tickets are heat-sensitive, and PCSO will not honor severely damaged tickets.

How to play the PCSO lotto games?

Meanwhile, playing all PCSO games comes with its respective rules. Below is a quick summary of how to play and win each game.

Ultra Lotto 6/58. The player wins if he/she gets the six (6) correct winning numbers from 1-58 in any order. Lotto enthusiasts can win exciting prizes, which may reach up to a billion pesos in jackpot prizes. The player may also bet with System Play for higher chances of winning. Here’s the summary of the fixed consolation prizes for Lotto 6/58:

Category	Winning Nos.	Prizes
1st Prize
(Initial Jackpot)	6 out of 6 Numbers	Php 49.5 Million
(net of agent’s prize commission)
2nd Prize	5 out of 6 Numbers	Php 120,000.00
3rd Prize	4 out of 6 Numbers	Php 2,000.00
4th Prize	3 out of 6 Numbers	Php 100.00

Grand Lotto 6/55. Select your lucky numbers from 1-55 and mark them on the playing card. The Lotto jackpot prize varies depending on ticket sales. However, players can expect considerable amounts as this is the second major game with the most significant initial jackpot prize of thirty (30) million pesos. Here’s the summary of consolation prizes:

Category	Winning Nos.	Prizes
1st Prize
(Initial Jackpot)	6 out of 6 Numbers	Php 29.7 Million
(net of agent’s prize commission)
2nd Prize	5 out of 6 Numbers	Php 100,000.00
3rd Prize	4 out of 6 Numbers	Php 1,500.00
4th Prize	3 out of 6 Numbers	Php 60.00

Super Lotto 6/49. It is one of the most popular PCSO lotto games. To win, the player must choose six correct numbers from 1-49 in any order. The jackpot prize for this game starts at P16,000,000. The player may also bet with System Play for higher chances of winning. Here’s the summary of the fixed consolation prizes for Lotto 6/49:

Category	Winning Nos.	Prizes
1st Prize
(Initial Jackpot)	6 out of 6 Numbers	Php 8.9 Million
(net of agent’s prize commission)
2nd Prize	5 out of 6 Numbers	Php 32,000.00
3rd Prize	4 out of 6 Numbers	Php 1,000.00
4th Prize	3 out of 6 Numbers	Php 30.00

Mega Lotto 6/45. Lotto players have to select their lucky numbers from 1-45. He/she wins if he/she gets the six winning numbers in any order. The jackpot prize for 6/45 starts at nine (9) million pesos. Additionally, 6/45 consolation prizes are now fixed. Here’s the summary:

Category	Winning Nos.	Prizes
1st Prize
(Initial Jackpot)	6 out of 6 Numbers	Php 15.8 Million
(net of agent’s prize commission)
2nd Prize	5 out of 6 Numbers	Php 50,000.00
3rd Prize	4 out of 6 Numbers	Php 1,200.00
4th Prize	3 out of 6 Numbers	Php 50.00

Lotto 6/42. For this significant game, players can select from numbers 1-42. The jackpot prize for 6/42 starts at six (6) million pesos. Players in any major game can also select LP or Lucky Pick if they want the machine to choose the numbers. Here’s the summary of consolation prizes:

Category	Winning Nos.	Prizes
1st Prize
(Initial Jackpot)	6 out of 6 Numbers	Php 5.9 Million
(net of agent’s prize commission)
2nd Prize	5 out of 6 Numbers	Php 24,000.00
3rd Prize	4 out of 6 Numbers	Php 800.00
4th Prize	3 out of 6 Numbers	Php 20.00
Swertres Lotto. Now referred to as 3D Lotto, it is one of PCSO’s most popular digit games. A player wins 4,500 (straight play) if he gets the exact winning numbers from 0-9. They may opt to play via the Rambolito System as well.
EZ2 Lotto. Presently referred to as 2D Lotto, players must choose their lucky numbers from 1-31. He/she wins if he/she has the exact winning combinations. The player also has the option to play via random pick or the Rambolito system.
STL Pares, Swer3, Swer2. Small Town Lottery (STL) is an exciting game for selected areas. To win the STL Pares, the player must have two (2) winning numbers from 1-40, in exact order. Playing the STL Swer3 is like playing the Swertres Lotto; the player wins if he gets the three (3) numbers from 0-9, in exact order, while for Swer2, the player wins if he/she gets the two (2) numbers, in exact order from 0-9.

About PCSO

PCSO is the central agency in the Philippines that legally conducts nationwide lottery draws. The money generated from the bets is used to provide funds for charity purposes. Likewise, the money is also utilized for health programs, medical assistance & services, and charities of national character. All PCSO lotto results for the major games can come in any order, while the digit games should be in exact order.

How to play the 3D lotto games?

For players to participate in the Swertres Lotto and claim their winnings, they must be at least 18 years old. Each ticket costs 10 pesos, which includes the 20% Documentary Stamp Tax (DST).

Players select three (3) numbers from 0-9 to play or opt for a lucky pick (LP) with a system-generated number. The game is won by matching the chosen numbers in the exact order.

In standard play, a player can win 4,500 pesos, while the Rambolito system offers the chance to win either 750 or 1,500 pesos.

Players are responsible for verifying the accuracy of their ticket information, including bet type, amount, draw date, and chosen numbers.

How to play the STL Swer3?

STL Swer3 mechanics are similar to Swertres Lotto, wherein a player can win the three (3) numbers from 0-9 (in exact order).

Players under eighteen (18) are not allowed to play any PCSO games.

Where to claim the Lotto Prize?

For E-Lotto claiming of prizes:

Php 300,000 and below can be cashed out through an e-lotto application. Prizes of Php 300,000.01 and above can be claimed at the PCSO Main Office.

To claim the prize, present the QR Code of the winning ticket and two (2) valid IDs.

To claim P5,000 pesos and below, prizes can be claimed at the nearest authorized lotto outlet or the nearest PCSO branch upon validation of the tickets.

For prizes above P5,000 pesos up to P20,000 pesos, prizes can be claimed at the nearest PCSO Branch office, while above P20,000 pesos, prizes can be claimed at the PCSO head office located at 605 Conservatory Bldg., Shaw Blvd. Corner Priceton St., Mandaluyong City.

Prizes of P10,000 pesos above are taxable (20%) under the TRAIN law.

Where to check the STL Swer3 Lotto Results?

Aside from lottopcso.com, players can watch the STL Swer3 results in real-time via PTV and their online accounts via Facebook and Twitter. Official PCSO accounts also air the lotto draws. On special occasions, such as critical national holidays, PCSO announces the suspension of draws days before the scheduled draw date.

Quick Tips for Lotto Players

Take extra good care of your Lotto ticket. Above all, don’t soak it underwater or subject it to high temperatures. Tickets are heat-sensitive, and PCSO will not accept tampered winning ticket (s).

Secure your ticket, and don’t give it to anyone you don’t trust for claiming.
