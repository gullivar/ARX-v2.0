Rice Research

Advancing Discovery and Innovation

Climate change. Water pollution. Increasing economic and social disparities. Rising rates of cancer and other diseases. The world is facing unprecedented challenges when it comes to human health and prosperity. At Rice, we are at the forefront of research aimed at advancing solutions to these fundamental problems.

SPECIAL RESEARCH EDITION

Research isn’t just a key driver of our mission — it’s the heartbeat of our contribution to society’s progress. From groundbreaking medical discoveries to innovative engineering solutions, the research and scholarship performed by our talented faculty, postdoctoral fellows and students transforms lives, strengthens our nation’s competitiveness and addresses global challenges.

Advancing Discovery Through Interdisciplinary Research

Rice University has focused on key areas of research, showcased through its interdisciplinary institutes and centers. These efforts drive progress in nanoscale science, computing, quantum studies, and collaborative research across biology, chemistry, and engineering. Together, they support excellent education, strengthen research facilities, foster collaboration, and help share scientific knowledge with students and the wider community.

WHERE OUR RESEARCH DOLLARS GO

Rice’s research funding fosters an ecosystem of discovery by supporting direct research costs, graduate student research, and indirect research expenses.

RESEARCH PROJECT STAFFING COSTS

Salaries, wages and benefits for researchers, staff and postdocs working on a research project

STUDENT SUMMER STIPENDS

Stipends for graduate students and undergraduate students with summer research positions working on a research project

RESEARCH EQUIPMENT & SUPPLIES

Lab equipment and research supplies specific to a research project

RESEARCH & CONFERENCE TRAVEL

Any travel costs for doing research and also travel to present project findings at conferences
