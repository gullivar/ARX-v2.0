Advance Home News Home News Scroll ABOUT ADVANCE S.I. Newhouse and his family founded Advance in 1922. After a century of investment and acquisitions, we remain a private, family-held business. Our mission is to build the value of the company over the long-term by fostering growth and innovation across a diversified portfolio which includes media, entertainment, communications, technology, education and other promising growth sectors. Advance’s portfolio of exceptional companies includes Condé Nast, Advance Local, Stage Entertainment, The IRONMAN Group, American City Business Journals, Leaders Group, Turnitin, and POP. Advance is also among the largest shareholders in Charter Communications, Reddit and Warner Bros. Discovery. Our track record of success has been made possible by cultivating true partnerships with our companies and their leaders — fostering the unique conditions necessary for organizations to create long-term value. OUR APPROACH At the heart of our approach are relationships built on trust and sustained by honesty, transparency, and relentless curiosity. We listen carefully to the teams at our companies in order to learn about their products, their concerns and insights, and the forces influencing their businesses. We play a supporting role — empowering their leadership, amplifying their strengths, and helping them fill their gaps. We are building for the future. We create long-term value by seeking out companies that can achieve financial success and are fundamentally doing work that we believe matters. We provide patient capital for the companies we believe in, supporting them in times of change and challenging them to achieve their potential.
