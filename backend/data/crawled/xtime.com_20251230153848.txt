XTIME FIXED OPS SOLUTIONS

Get the most out of your service department with Xtime fixed ops solutions that help you deliver a top-notch customer experience. You’ll be able to service more vehicles faster, help your team work more efficiently, and increase your service revenue. That means happier employees, more satisfied customers, and a more profitable dealership with nearly 50% more dollars per RO.

SCHEDULE

Schedule makes it easy for customers to book available service openings from their phone or computer. Manage the workload in your shop to service more cars in less time while earning 27x return on your auto shop management software investment. Build trust with customers by showing them what services you recommend, how much they will cost, and ways to finance them if needed.

ENGAGE

Engage helps customers save time with personalized, fast check-in options and personalized communication. You can text updates throughout the service visit, and customers can pay online before their service is completed so they can check out faster and move on with their day.

+

INVITE

Invite is your service marketing tool to help bring customers back to your dealership. Send personalized messages and offers based on past visits and declined repairs. This helps fill empty shop time, boost repair orders, and increase profits by reaching the right customers at the right time.

INSPECT

Inspect helps you provide a comprehensive vehicle inspection and faster service recommendation approvals. Service technicians can take and send customers clear pictures and videos of needed repairs, making it easier for customers to say yes. It also helps staff work better together and increase repair dollars.
