Start Live Chat Live Chat available weekdays, 7:00 am - 6:30 pm CT Call us at 1-800-227-2345 Available any time of day or night Our highly trained specialists are available 24/7 via phone and on weekdays can assist through online chat. We connect patients, caregivers, and family members with essential services and resources at every step of their cancer journey. Home Get Involved Fundraise Fundraise: Be the ACS in Your Community The American Cancer Society (ACS) mission needs you now more than ever! YOU can make a difference today, individually, or by leading your community to take up the fight against cancer. There are endless new and creative opportunities to raise funds or get involved with the ACS, and you are empowered to make it happen. Start here to create “your ACS” today! Want to make the most impact? Fundraise with Facebook! Raise more funds by creating a Facebook Fundraiser. It's a fun, easy way to help save lives. Connect with Facebook Fundraise in Your Community Raise funds for the American Cancer Society's vision to end cancer as we know it, for everyone. Want to be part of something bigger? Please join us for one our signature special events or outreach programs hosted in cities across the nation! Find an Activity Near You Relay For Life® More than a walk, the American Cancer Society's signature event rallies communities to help end cancer as we know it, for everyone. Making Strides Against Breast Cancer® This powerful movement unites communities nationwide to help end breast cancer as we know it. Men Wear Pink This distinguished group of community leaders is committed to raising funds and awareness to support anyone, male or female, who is undergoing treatment today, and those who may face a diagnosis in the future. Galas Communities across the country host a variety of events that offer a fun and festive way to help improve the lives of people facing cancer their families. Golf Tournaments Our golf events provide players of all skill levels an outstanding golf experience and the opportunity to support our mission in local communities. Endurance/Fitness Events Become the driving force behind our fight against cancer by competing in your favorite sporting event. Whether running, cycling, skiing, hiking, swimming or even kayaking, you can help us fight the unthinkable by being unstoppable. Coaches vs. Cancer Coaches, teams, and fans are empowered to fight cancer by participating in awareness efforts, advocacy programs, and fundraising activities. Fundraise Your Way No matter how you choose to raise funds, we’ve got the tools and support you need for fundraising success. All you need is creativity, passion, and imagination. Here are just a few ways you can get involved: Start Your Own Fundraiser Turn your passion into purpose. Start a fundraiser today and help the American Cancer Society end cancer as we know it, for everyone. Connect with Facebook Raise more funds by creating a Facebook Fundraiser. It’s a fun, easy way to help save lives. Do A Livestream Help us fight against cancer with the Gamers vs. Cancer program, which gives the gaming community meaningful opportunities to give back through partnerships, events like esports tournaments, and charity live streaming. Learn about Gamers vs. Cancer Create a Tribute Creating an online honor or memorial page gives friends and family a powerful way to celebrate your loved one's unique story. This is also a great opportunity to raise funds to fuel our work to end cancer as we know it, for everyone. Play It Forward The American Cancer Society is giving gamers a new way to make a difference! Play your favorite games and Besitos will match your winnings to support cancer research and patient care. Every game played brings us one step closer to a world without cancer. Virtual Challenges An easy and convenient way to help fund the American Cancer Society’s work to ensure everyone has an opportunity to prevent, detect, treat, and survive cancer. Youth & Young Professionals Young Professionals Young Professional volunteers improve the lives of cancer patients and their families through advocacy, research, and patient support through mission-based activities and fundraising that create a lasting impact. American Cancer Society On Campus American Cancer Society On Campus chapters engage middle school, high school, and college students across the country to support our mission through campus activities focusing on advocacy, discovery, patient support, and fundraising. Youth Sports There are plenty of opportunities for sports teams to get engaged in the fight against cancer. From football, basketball, soccer, baseball, softball, hockey, volleyball, and other sports, there is a way for any team to become involved. Gold Together Funds raised through the #GoldTogether movement directly support childhood cancer research, education, advocacy, and service, as well as promote cancer prevention efforts targeting children to help them reduce their risk for cancer later in life. Schools vs. Cancer The American Cancer Society Schools vs. Cancer  program brings education and community service to an elementary classroom in a fun and exciting way that also allows students to help save lives from cancer. Corporate Engagement Learn how your organization can make a difference in your community and beyond by supporting our lifesaving mission. CEOs Against Cancer® This influential group of corporate and business leaders brings the passion, expertise, and resources needed to change the course of cancer in our workplaces, communities, and beyond. Become a Sponsor Your organization can make a real impact by raising awareness and funds to help us ensure everyone has an opportunity to prevent, detect, treat, and survive cancer. Employee Engagement Provide opportunities for your employees to come together in the fight against cancer. Need Help? Questions and Support Contact us or call us at 800.227.2345 if you need help navigating your options, or if you have questions.
