About Us The AnonDNS allows you to register own name in zone .anondns.net for your dynamic IP address
        without providing any personal data. Advantages We don't require your name, email, phone or other personal data. No need to register!
        Just register a new name and get a key (token) to manage it! To update a registered record no special software is required! You could use curl , wget or your favourite browser.
        Just retrieve a special URL via HTTPS. Donations If you would like to donate to this project just send Bitcoins , Litecoins or money via PayPal . Copyright Vitalii Druzhinin , 2014-2020.
