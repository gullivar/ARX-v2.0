Student feedback made actionable
Boost response rates, analyze results instantly, and turn insights into meaningful improvements.

Watermark Course Evaluations & Surveys streamlines how higher education institutions gather and use student feedback. From easy administration to AI-powered analysis, get the reliable insights you need to elevate teaching, learning, and the student experience, all in one platform.

Go beyond course evaluations

Integrate with your LMS to easily meet students where they learn.
Surveys are mobile-friendly so you can engage students on the go.

Integrates with your ecosystem

Course Evaluations & Surveys works with your existing LMS, SIS, and Watermark solutions:

Ellucian Banner & Colleague

Automatically sync and refresh course, student, TA, and instructor data from your SIS to streamline course evaluation setup and ensure data integrity.

Anthology Blackboard

Source and refresh foundational data from Blackboard for fast and reliable course evaluation setup. Drive student engagement with embedded surveys, notifications, and more – all in the LMS.

Canvas

Connect Course Evaluations & Surveys with Canvas to build accurate survey projects fast. Get survey links to students right where they are. Promote responses with notifications and options like gradebook points and grade blocking.

Learn more
D2L Brightspace

Tap Brightspace data to simplify course evaluation setup and ensure survey accuracy. Provide students seamless access to surveys, and connect instructors to their results directly from their LMS.

Moodle

Pull course and enrollment data to build your course evaluations and give students one-click access to surveys, straight from Moodle.

Watermark Faculty Success

By automatically linking student feedback to faculty reviews, the integration saves time for both faculty and administrators and ensures the integrity of evaluation data.

Watermark Planning & Self-Study

Leverage indirect measures of the student experience from Course Evaluations & Surveys to gain a complete, actionable view of performance for continuous improvement.
