Frequent Asked Questions

What programs does XtraMath offer and how do I choose the right one for my students?

XtraMath offers addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and multiple custom programs. You can select based on the students’ grade level and learning needs.

How can I track my student’s progress?

Teachers get detailed reports showing each student’s accuracy, speed, and fluency level for every operation.

Can I change a student’s program after assigning it?

Yes, you can restart or switch a student’s program at any time from the teacher dashboard.

What’s included in the Premium version?

Premium includes enhanced reports, customization options, out-of-school access, family reports, and other teacher-friendly features. Explore XtraMath Premium.

Do you meet Web Content Accessibility Guildelines (WCAG)?

Yes, XtraMath is WCAG 2.1 AA compliant.

Is student data safe on XtraMath?

Yes. XtraMath is committed to student privacy and complies with COPPA, FERPA, and other student data protection laws. We do not sell or share student data with third parties.
