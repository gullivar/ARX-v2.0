Advancing light. Learn more about SPIE SPIE is the international society for optics and photonics. Serving a global membership of over 25,000, we bring engineers, scientists, students, and business professionals together to advance light-based science and technology. Over the past five years, we have invested more than $26 million in the international optics community through our advocacy and support, including scholarships, educational resources, travel grants, endowed gifts, and public-policy development.
