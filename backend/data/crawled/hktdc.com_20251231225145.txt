Hong Kong Advantages
As the gateway to the Chinese Mainland and the most international city in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, Hong Kong can help businesses realise their growth and expansion plans, including helping mainland firms go global and overseas companies expand across Asia and beyond.
Business-friendly environment, free economy
Robust and efficient financial ecosystem
Proximity to the Chinese Mainland and wider Asia region
Low and simple tax system, one of the most taxfriendly systems globally
Free flow of information, goods, capital and people
Robust legal and IP infrastructure, the only Chinese city practicing common law
Rule of law and level-playing field
World-class professional services with broad knowledge on global markets

How HKTDC Can Help
Build your business
Explore key markets
Expand your business
Get market insights

The HKTDC empowers SMEs and start-ups via practical programmes and advisory support to accelerate their growth. Businesses can tap into tailored resources to build a strong foundation and scale up with confidence.

T-box Programme

A free 3-month support programme for SMEs to achieve their transformation goals in branding, digital transformation, manufacturing and supply chain solutions, new markets and sustainability

SME Funding information

Information on accessing Government funding schemes that support the development of businesses in Hong Kong.

HKStartup Society

A community platform for start-ups to connect, collaborate and share resources. It fosters peer learning and brand visibility.

Start-up Express

A flagship entrepreneurship development programme offering exposure and mentorship and facilitating global market access through pitching and networking events.
