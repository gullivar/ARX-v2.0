KeenDNS service provides you with a permanent customizable domain name that makes services running on your Keenetic router accessible from the Internet. Dynamic or non-external IP address? ISP is blocking HTTP(S) port? Not a problem anymore!
With KeenDNS you're able to set up public or restricted access to particular applications on and services of your home network, using a name of your choice, e.g.: sunnydale.keenetic.link.
