Classic Football Shirts – Original Vintage Kits, Official Reissue Jerseys & Current Season Releases

Welcome to Classic Football Shirts – the ultimate destination for vintage football shirts, retro soccer jerseys and classic kits. We are the home of football shirts, with over 100,000 products in stock, spanning from the 1970s to 2025. From heritage club strips to modern future classics, this is where football lives.

Founded by fans, for fans – Classic Football Shirts was built on a love for the game and a deep respect for its culture. Whether you're a lifelong collector or starting your journey into the world of football culture, there’s something for everyone - from the classic stripes of Serie A and La Liga giants to the adrenaline-soaked designs of the Barclays Premier League era. Printed legends, player name sets, and names and numbers from football’s greatest moments all live here.

We proudly cover the most iconic eras in football history - from the golden days of the Barclays Premier League to international tournaments and European club dominance. Our collection spans shirts from the game's greatest European and global legends like Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Diego Maradona, through to the unforgettable ‘Barclaysmen’ era of swagger and cult heroes: Adel Taarabt, Jay-Jay Okocha, Morten Gamst Pedersen, and Yakubu.

Beyond players, we also celebrate the visual language of football - iconic shirt sponsors like Newcastle Brown Ale, Vodafone, Opel and Pirelli define some of the most beloved kits of all time. From Champions League classics to international tournament greats, from match-worn rarities to printed shirts with official player names, our collection is trusted by collectors, stylists, historians and fans worldwide.

Our personal vault includes one of the world’s largest private archives of match-worn shirts – shared with over 3 million followers across our social media channels. Featuring football’s most legendary shirts, including Cristiano Ronaldo’s Sporting CP jersey before joining Manchester United, this vault is a love letter to the game.

Explore official reissues, cult club classics, retro training wear, and clearance teamwear. But it doesn’t stop there - our global range lets you uncover shirts from teams you may never have heard of, hidden gems and forgotten kits waiting to be rediscovered. There’s a sense of mystery and thrill in exploring the unknown - the perfect experience for football fans, collectors, discover teams from around the globe you may have never even heard of.

Fast worldwide shipping, thousands of new additions daily, and one mission: to help you wear the legacy, build your collection, and live the culture. Classic Football Shirts is where football’s past meets its future - printed, stitched and remembered.
