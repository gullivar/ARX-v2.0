Privacy & Security All scripts and resources are executed within a sandboxed environment, ensuring that cross-site tracking cannot be performed. Ad-Shield provides sandbox technology that enables sites to run their business logic in a secure and isolated execution environment. This technology prevents cross-site tracking by design, as each sandbox instance is isolated and cryptographic keys are automatically rotated on a regular basis.
