What is Vidu? Vidu is a cutting-edge AI video generator that transforms text and images into high-quality videos, supporting multiple creation modes: Text to Video, Image to Video, and Reference to Video. Designed for independent creators and teams, Vidu simplifies the production of high-quality videos, 2D animations, and diverse artistic styles, empowering users to bring their creative visions to life effortlessly. With robust semantic understanding and fast generation speed, Vidu eliminates production complexities, allowing creators to focus solely on their ideas. From low resolution to stunning 1080p outputs, Vidu offers unmatched flexibility and efficiency, providing a broader canvas for creativity. What is the use of Vidu AI? Vidu AI is a powerful tool for creating high-quality AI-generated videos with exceptional consistency and fast generation speed. It is widely used across various industries, from social media content creation to professional video production and commercial advertising. Many creators use Vidu AI to produce viral videos like kissing, hugging, or fighting clips. Film studios and animation teams leverage it for studio-level 2D animations and cinematic content. Businesses also use Vidu AI to enhance marketing videos, such as image-to-video to animate product images or reference-to-video to change ad backgrounds, making video advertisements more dynamic and engaging. With its advanced AI capabilities, Vidu AI streamlines video production for creators, studios, and businesses. What's the difference between Vidu's Reference to Video and Image to Video? Image to Video: Vidu's Image-to-Video feature allows users to create AI-generated videos from a static image, bringing them to life with motion and animation. Users can also set the first and last frames of their AI video. It streamlines the video or animation creation process—often leading to surprising and creative results. Reference to Video: Reference to Video lets users upload three or more images of an object, character, or scene as references, and Vidu can combine elements into a seamless video based on a prompt. Vidu's Reference to Video also supports uploading different angles of a character or object to ensure consistency. Vidu also offers the My References feature, allowing users to save characters, props, and scenes for effortless reuse, enhancing both creative efficiency and video quality. This feature is ideal for maintaining consistency and seamlessly generating videos that blend multiple visual references. Is Vidu AI free to use?
