Dstny provides cloud-based telephony and communication solutions that help businesses collaborate, connect, and deliver great customer experiences, wherever their teams work. Who uses Dstny? We support businesses of all sizes across Europe, from small teams to large enterprises, through local Dstny teams, service providers and resellers. What does “make hybrid work” mean? Hybrid work means being able to work from the office, home, or anywhere in between. Dstny helps businesses create a flexible setup where communication stays simple, seamless, and secure. How does Dstny support hybrid work? We help businesses break free from location-bound communication, like fixed landline phones. With Dstny’s cloud-based solution, you can call, collaborate, and stay connected from anywhere, on any device. Our telephony integrates seamlessly with CRM systems and collaboration tools such as Microsoft Teams, making teamwork across locations effortless and consistent. What is UCaaS? UCaaS stands for Unified Communications as a Service. Simply put, cloud-based tools for communication, gathered into one platform. What makes Dstny different from other communication providers? Dstny focuses on mobility, simplicity, and local service. Our mobile-firs approach helps teams stay connected anyhwere. And with our integrations, people can work mwithout switching between multiple apps. Does Dstny integrate with tools like Microsoft Teams? Yes. Dstny provides native voice integrations for Microsoft Teams and other collaboration platforms like Zoom and Webex. How does Dstny ensure secure communication? Our cloud solutions follow strict compliance and security standards, using encrypted connections and secure data handling across all services. Can I use Dstny on my mobile phone? Absolutely. Mobile-first communication is core to the Dstny experience, work calls, group numbers, and collaboration are available directly on your smartphone. What industries benefit from Dstny? Dstny supports everything from retail and healthcare to logistics and professional services. Basically any business that values reliable, flexible communication.
