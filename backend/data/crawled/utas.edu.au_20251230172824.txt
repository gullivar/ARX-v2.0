Leading the way for a sustainable future

The University of Tasmania has ranked #1 on climate action for universities internationally.

For the third year running, we are officially the tertiary sector's world-leader in taking climate action, with the prestigious Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings rating us number one in climate action globally for 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025.
