equipment. Buyers can find used truck listings from hundreds of manufacturers, including Chevrolet, Ford, Freightliner, GMC, Hino, International, Isuzu, Kenworth, Mack, Peterbilt, and Volvo. Everything from light-duty to Class 8 trucks, including conventional sleeper trucks and day cabs, bucket trucks, tank trucks, fuel trucks, van trucks, and much more are all represented on TruckPaper.com. Buyers can also find used trailers from Dorsey, Fontaine, Great Dane, MAC Trailer Mfg., Transcraft, Utility, Wabash, and many other manufacturers. TruckPaper.com offers quick and easy access to thousands of for-sale listings, complete with specs, photo galleries, videos, and seller contact information. The website makes it simple for buyers to find the trucks and trailers they need, whether browsing categories or manufacturers or using the site’s powerful search features. For sellers, TruckPaper.com provides an intuitive platform for listing trucks and trailers for sale. Truck Paper magazine, meanwhile, is published weekly in multiple regional editions, enabling sellers to reach specific markets. TruckPaper.com is a service of Sandhills Global, headquartered in Lincoln, Nebraska, USA. Sandhills is the company behind Machinery Trader, TractorHouse, AuctionTime.com, and many other platforms and publications serving buyers and sellers in the construction, agriculture, trucking, and aviation industries.
