ABOUT US

Tekrom Technology, established in 2003 and spanning on 25 years of experience in informatics sector, is marketing eCommerce Systems and Web Technologies under its “T-Soft” brand.

Innovation has never slowed down in Tekrom Technology. We are now offering eCommerce applications with more than 230 staff, consist mainly from software developers and technical support teams, such as; web and mobile eCommerce services, web themes, web page design services, several payment options, campaign modules etc...

In addition to aforementioned eCommerce offers, we are now developing cutting edge technology applications such as; data analysis, augmented reality, social commerce (sCommerce), professional integration management application, automatic translation engines, bespoke IT solutions and dashboard programs.

We love software development using new technologies.
