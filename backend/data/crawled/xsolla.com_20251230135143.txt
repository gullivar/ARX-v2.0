What is NPS?NPS is a market research metric based on a single survey question: how likely are you to recommend a company, product, or service to a friend or colleague?

Responses are grouped into promoters (9-10), passives (7-8), and detractors (0-6).
How is NPS calculated?The Net Promoter Score is calculated by subtracting the percentage of detractors from the percentage of promoters collected by the survey.
What does your total NPS mean?The score ranges from -100 to +100. Any score above 0 indicates more promoters than detractors.
Above 0 is good.
Above 30 is favorable.
Above 50 is excellent.
Above 80 is world-class.
