Welcome to Joie – Your Trusted Partner in Parenting



At Joie, we believe that every journey with your little one should be safe, smooth and joyful. Our range of baby gear—including car seats, pushchairs, and travel systems—is designed to meet the highest safety standards while offering convenience and comfort for both parents and children.



Why Choose Joie?
Safety First: Our products are rigorously tested to exceed EU safety standards, ensuring your child's protection on every adventure.
Innovative Designs: From multi-mode prams to spinning car seats, our products are crafted to adapt to your growing child's needs.
Affordable Quality: We offer premium baby gear at prices that won't break the bank, making top-notch safety and comfort accessible to all families.
Explore Our Range
Joie Signature: From elegant prams and strollers to ultra safe car seats and thoughtfully designed travel systems, our Joie Signature collection of upgraded, luxury baby gear is carefully crafted with premium materials to create products that last for years and make parenthood stylish, seamless and safe.
Car Seats: From snug rearward-facing baby car seats that cradle your newborn with care to adaptable multi-stage car seats that grow with your little one and belted booster seats that provide security for older children, every Joie car seat delivers powerful protection with thoughtful features.
Pushchairs: From robust and full-featured prams with lie-flat options and spacious storage, to lightweight strollers that are compact and easy to manoeuvre, to versatile travel systems that combine essential gear for seamless transitions, Joie’s pushchair range offers options to suit every family’s needs.
Swings & Bouncers: Whether you are looking for a compact rocker to soothe your baby during quiet moments or a multi-motion swing to keep them entertained, Joie has just the product for you and your little one. Our range of bouncers and swings are designed with both functionality and style in mind, with features like adjustable settings, plush padding and easy portability they provide the ultimate convenience and comfort.
Cots & Cribs: Keep your newborn close with our bedside cribs, providing a safe and comfortable sleeping environment right next to your bed. These cribs make night feeds and comforting your baby easier, ensuring restful sleep for both of you. For families on the go, our lightweight and easy-to-set-up travel cots offer a cosy place for your baby to sleep, whether visiting family or on holiday. With Joie's bedside cribs and travel cots, enjoy peace of mind and convenience, knowing your baby is always close and comfortable.
Baby Carriers: Our ergonomic and easy to use baby carriers are made for hugging, not lugging. Suitable from birth, our carriers are designed so you can get out and about with your baby.
Highchairs: Featuring quick cleanups, multi-modes and compact folds, our highchairs make mealtimes easier for you and your family.
Join the Joie community today and experience the joy of worry-free parenting with products that are built to last and designed with love.
