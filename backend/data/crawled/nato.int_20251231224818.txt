NATO is a political and military alliance of countries from Europe and North America. Its members are committed to protecting each other from any threat. Learn more Member countries NATO Allies are sovereign states that come together through NATO to discuss political and security issues and make collective decisions by consensus. Organization NATO’s purpose is to guarantee the freedom and security of its members through political and military means. Secretary General The Secretary General is the Alliance’s top international civil servant, responsible for steering the process of consultation and decision-making in the Alliance.
