E-Commerce in Rural Areas of Nigeria
October 9, 2025

Rural e-commerce is accelerating in Nigeria, with over half of Jumia orders now coming from secondary cities and remote areas. With over 350 Pickup Stations nationwide and a network of 67 logistics partners, Jumia is expanding access and last-mile efficiency, connecting underserved communities to the digital economy.
