Terence Stamp Passes Away Aged 87 ‚Äî August 17, 2025 Terence Stamp , a veteran English actor with a career in film spanning six decades, has passed away this morning. Stamp voiced Mankar Camoran , one of the main antagonists in Oblivion . He was also known for his role as General Zod in Superman , and as a favor to Todd Howard said " Kneel before Todd " (seen in the Making of Oblivion Documentary ) in the same tone as his iconic line from the film. His performances have earned him Golden Globe and Cannes Film Festival awards as well as Oscar and BAFTA nominations. "He leaves behind an extraordinary body of work, both as an actor and a writer that will continue to touch people for years to come," his family stated. Stamp had only done about ten voice roles throughout his career, but his performance as Mankar Camoran is widely considered one of the most iconic roles in the franchise to date. He will be dearly missed. Julian LeFay Passes Away Aged 59 ‚Äî July 22, 2025 Elder Scrolls co-creator Julian LeFay , the core game director, programmer, and designer at Bethesda Softworks in the 1980s and '90s, has passed away tonight. LeFay was considered the chief imagineer behind The Elder Scrolls series during his tenure of the studio, being the project director behind Arena , Daggerfall , and Battlespire , contributing to the programming, gameplay mechanics, and lore design of the series. An in-depth statement has been posted by OnceLost Games, a company Julian co-founded, as well as a tribute posted by Bethesda, penned by Julian's friend and successor Todd Howard . We would like to wish him peace, and for him to know he will not be forgotten. ZeniMax and Virtuos Hit by Major Layoffs ‚Äî July 17, 2025 ZeniMax Online Studios , the developers of The Elder Scrolls Online , has been affected by a sweeping round of layoffs done inside Microsoft. On July 2, Microsoft announced they were shelving an unannounced MMO at ZOS, affecting over 200 developers. Matt Firor , the founder and head of the studio, also announced his departure from the company, being succeeded by Jo Burba on July 14. The studio had been working on a new AAA game codenamed Project Blackbird since 2018, with many Elder Scrolls Online alumni being tasked to work on the project. According to reports, development on the game had been coming along smoothly, with a release date set for 2028, and its gameplay impressed executives at Xbox. The cancellation of the game by Microsoft came as an unexpected decision to the team, most of whom have since been laid off. The ZOS United-CWA union has since released a statement regarding concerns with pay and benefits related to the layoffs. Virtuos, the studio handling development on Oblivion Remastered , also announced a series of layoffs on July 17. Approximately 7% of Virtuos' workforce worldwide was affected due to the layoffs. We at the Unofficial Elder Scrolls Pages wish all the talented developers affected by the layoffs the best of luck in their future endeavors, and thank them for all their hard work in making the world of The Elder Scrolls come to life. Tamriel Rebuilt Releases Grasping Fortune ‚Äî May 1, 2025 Tamriel Rebuilt is a mod for Morrowind which adds the mainland of the province to the game, piece by piece. Today marks the release of version 25.05, Grasping Fortune , which centers on the Hlaalu capital of Narsis , a nexus of immense wealth and cruel injustice. Surrounding the city is the vast and dangerous hinterland of Shipal-Shin , whereas a number of Hlaalu and Redoran towns dot the shores of the Thirr River in the Coronati Basin region downstream of Narsis. Features include: 410 brand-new exterior cells of landmass ‚Äì this is about a third the size of vanilla's Vvardenfell island. Aside from the vast metropolis of Narsis, the area includes the desert canyons of the Shipal-Shin region, the densely settled agricultural hinterland of the Coronati Basin, and the canopied mushroom forest of the Othreleth Woods. Over 870 new cells of interior locations , of which slightly less than 200 are for Narsis alone. Another ~140 are hostile dungeons of several variants: in addition to vanilla dungeon types, there are also the brand new Skyrender hives, an Ayleid dungeon, and a Remanite fortress. Over 270 new quests , featuring questlines for nearly all vanilla factions ‚Äì most especially House Hlaalu with over 40 new quests, including a Grandmaster sequence ‚Äì but also the brand-new Ja-Natta Syndicate, an unscrupulous criminal faction challenging the local Thieves Guild. Download links and detailed information about new features are available on Tamriel Rebuilt's website .
