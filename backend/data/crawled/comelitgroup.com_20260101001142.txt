Discover more 0 + Collaborators 0 Subsidiaries worldwide 0 R&D centers 0 Milion Turnover (2024) Founded in 1956 by four entrepreneurs in a small garage in Bergamo, Comelit began with a vision to make innovation and security solutions accessible to all. Today, with strong roots in our community, we’ve grown into a global company, operating in 96 countries with over 1,000 employees. Our family-led Group includes 14 companies, with one central headquarters, 9 subsidiaries across 5 continents, 8 research and development centres, and 5 representative offices in key markets. Guided by the same spirit of innovation, we continue to unite technology and humanity to deliver security solutions that make the world safer for everyone.
