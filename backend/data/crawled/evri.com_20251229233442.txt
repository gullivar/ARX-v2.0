Send, track and return parcels

At Evri, we provide cheap and convenient parcel delivery services, using our network of over 18,000 friendly parcel Couriers.

We deliver over 800 million packages a year to over 220 countries and territories worldwide.

Use our Courier service online to send, track and return your parcels quickly and easily - all in one place. Just choose from our ParcelShops, Lockers or book a Courier Collection.

You can track parcels every step of the way using our website or App, and full tracking is included as standard with every parcel.

Why Evri?
Complete flexibility

Send or return your parcels with our network of parcel Couriers, using one of 10,000+ ParcelShops and Lockers located across the UK. Or, book door-to-door Couriers online for collection throughout the country.
