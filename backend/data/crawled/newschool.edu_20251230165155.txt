Meet Our ChangeMakers
Darrick Hamilton: Embedding Values and Equity into Economics

University Professor Hamilton founded and leads a groundbreaking New School institute focused on equitable economic policy.

Oscar '18: Leveraging Tech to Bolster Communities and Democracy

Oscar, an MA International Affairs grad, is the NYC Civic Engagement Commission's chief information officer, a role enabling him to develop tech solutions that make govenment work smarter for immigrant populations.

Stefania Bulbarella '19: Projecting New Voices in Theater

Bulbarella, a recent BFA Dramatic Arts graduate, has risen quickly to Broadway stardom in stage projection, drawing on the connections and skills she developed in the College of Performing Arts at The New School.

Bhawani Venkataraman: Collaborating on Science-Led Change

Eugene Lang College professor Venkataraman employs science to highlight the links between resources, politics, and equity.

Shannon: Making College Life a Little More Sustainable

Inspired by her Lang courses, BA Screen Studies sophomore Shannon launched an initiative to make dorm move-outs an opportunity to hand down furnishings instead of send them to the landfill.
