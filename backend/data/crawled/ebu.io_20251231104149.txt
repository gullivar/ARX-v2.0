          adoption of OAuth 2.0, the widespread use of cloud infrastructures and
          the advancement of other web technologies, ebu.io has become
          redundant. In 2022, the ebu.io projects 'home' and technical modules
          have been removed for all projects, except for the RadioDNS project. Further note that the EBU QC Tool and the EBU Gitlab repositories
