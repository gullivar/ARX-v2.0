Sienna Spiro

Pop, Dance-Pop, Neo-Soul, Contemporary R&B

Spotify - New Music Friday: SIENNA SPIRO - Die On This Hill

Like many would-be singers, Sienna Spiro has put in work interpreting songs on social media with her own soul-pop-affected voice. Unlike most, though, that work paid off in a sudden burst of success, including the minor U.K. hit “Maybe.” and the debut EP Sink Now, Swim Later. She continued releasing singles, finding success with standouts like "You Stole the Show" and "Die On This Hill." Born in London in 2005, Spiro spent her teen years sharing her takes on various covers and original songs in short-form, bite-sized videos on social media apps. AllMusic Staff Pick – December 29, 2025
Emmylou Harris
Elite Hotel
December, 1975
Country-Rock
While Emmylou Harris spent much of her career carrying on the legacy of Gram Parsons, Elite Hotel, released 50 years ago today, ranks among her most overt tributes to his genius, thanks to its covers of the Flying Burrito Brothers' "Sin City" and "Wheels," along with "Ooh Las Vegas" from the Grievous Angel album.

- Jason Ankeny
