ResNexus equips you with the tools to easily manage your hospitality business with a single login.
Everything at a Glance

Property Management System
View and manage all of your existing and potential reservations in one place and reduce double bookings. With our PMS system everything syncs automatically to save you time and stress, with comprehensive filtering so you can easily see the information you need, when you need to see it.
Get Found, Get Booked
OTA Marketing Channels
Get found and get booked with our channel manager which provides direct connections to top OTAs like Expedia, Booking.com, Airbnb, TripAdvisor, Vrbo, and Google Hotel ads. Our Premier edition even allows you to enjoy commission-free OTA bookings! Easily manage rates, availability, minimum nights, and more, all from the ResNexus system. Take advantage of the billboard effect and generate more direct bookings.
Custom, Professional, & Optimized
ResNexus Websites
A quality website is the guest's portal to making direct, online bookings, and to learning more about what makes your property unique. Have our professional website design team custom-build an SEO-optimized, ADA-compliant website according to your needs.
Intuitive Booking

ResNexus Booking Engine
When it comes to the guests, providing a frictionless booking experience is paramount. Making an online reservation should be visually appealing and intuitive, and that's exactly what the ResNexus booking engine does. Clearly display your gorgeous property and availability, and make add-on product recommendations on the retail sidebar.
Guest Convenience
ResNexus Door Locks
Enhance the guest experience with automatic Wi-Fi door locks. This feature pairs well with contactless check-in, allowing your guests to choose how they want to interact with your business. Save on staffing resources and please your guests with the convenience of being able to access their rooms hassle-free.
Protect Your Business
Travel Protection
Protect your business from last-minute cancellations due to death, illness, or injury with our exclusive Worry-Free Bookings™ integration. When a guest can't make their reservation for one of the aforementioned reasons, Worry-Free Bookings™ refunds them for their full booking total, and also reimburses your property for that same amount.

Fast, Painless, Secure

Payment Processing
Payment processing is fast, painless, and secure, with GDPR and PCI certification. All major credit cards are accepted, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover, as well as PayPal. Rest easy knowing that you and your guests' information is secure.

Maximize Profits, Year-Round
Revenue Management
Make use of our integrated dynamic pricing techniques such as automated yield management, sliding rates, and minimum nights to form a rate management strategy and maximize your profits.
