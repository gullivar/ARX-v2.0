Yugipedia is a free repository on every aspect of the Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise—the card game, anime and manga series, video games, and everything in-between—that anyone can edit. Since May 26, 2005, we've worked hard to bring the most complete and comprehensive database to you, and we're still growing, even 199,567 articles later. Anyone and everyone is free to contribute, so create an account or log in on an existing account to get started. If you're an old user from the FANDOM wiki, you can also import your account to reclaim your old edits.
