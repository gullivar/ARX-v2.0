Designing drones that can fly in air ducts New research published in npj Robotics addresses the challenge of flying small quadrotors in air ducts as small as 35 cm (14 inches). Congratulations to Taous Iatariene, PhD student in the Multispeech team, supervised by Alexandre Guérin and Romain Serizel, who received a Best Student Paper Award at the Acusticum 2025 Forum. The prize was awarded for her paper Tracking of Intermittent and Moving Speakers: Dataset and Metrics , delivered by the EAA European Acoustics Association at the Forum, held from 23 to 26 June in Málaga, Spain.
