DG Systems, CBIC has implemented Digital Signature Masking and Document Watermarking features on eSanchit documents. Due to these changes, users may observe a ‘Question Mark’ or ‘Cross Mark’ on the digitally signed (masked) portion of the document during viewing. Now, while making NEFT/RTGS payments, details of CPIN, Virtual IFSC Code, Total Duty Amount, Account Name and Challan Expiry Date will be auto-populated on the net-banking portal after login, eliminating the need to manually copy and paste payment details.
