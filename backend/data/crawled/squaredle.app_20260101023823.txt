Squares fade out once you find all the words using them. Find all the
          words to clear the board! Tap the word count to see which words you've found and how long the remaining words are. Capitalized or hyphenated words
          usually aren't in our word list. If it helps, you can rotate the board with the rotate icon for another perspective. What's the progress bar? Longer words are worth more points, bringing you closer to unlocking
          the next perk . When you reach the first star ,
              tiles show how many words start with them . The second star reveals how many words use each tile . Get to the third to unlock hints in the word list. The globe icon represents the average of everyone's score so far. Try to beat it! When does the next puzzle come out? New puzzles come out each day at 3am Pacific. Monday puzzles are the easiest; Fridays are the hardest.
          Saturday puzzles have fun twists, and Sundays have tons of words. Each day also brings a new 3x3 Express puzzle. Can I use a physical keyboard? Yes! Type out the letters, then press < Enter > to

Bonus word You've found a bonus word ! (In the tutorial puzzle, all words except the tutorial words are treated
      as "bonus" words.) Bonus words make the base game easier by excluding obscure or questionable
      words. Finding them won't progress your score, but they show up on the
      leaderboards. Bonus words include: Archaic, obscure or domain-specific words eyne, arty, graviton Non-American English words colour, tyre, brae Some slang holla, nosh There are usually about 2x more bonus words than required words. Bonus
      words ensure that puzzles remain accessible to all, while recognizing
