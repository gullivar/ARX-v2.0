Learn more Frequently Asked Questions Is Linen Chest a Canadian company? Yes, Linen Chest is a Canadian, family-owned retailer specializing in home goods. Founded in 1961 in Montreal, Quebec, the company has grown to over 35 stores across Canada. Linen Chest offers a wide selection of bedding, bathroom accessories, kitchenware, home décor, and custom window treatments—helping Canadians create beautiful, stylish and comfortable homes from coast to coast. Who owns Linen Chest in Montreal? Linen Chest was founded in Montreal in 1961 by Sylvia Leibner. Today, it remains proudly family-owned and operated by her sons, Sheldon and Stanley Leibner.
