Arnold Transit Company was founded in 1878 by George T. Arnold as the Arnold & Coats Ferry Line. Coal-fire steamboats transported passengers and goods for almost 70 years to various Michigan ports and islands. The company changed its name in 1890 and was acquired by Prentiss M. Brown in 1935. Huron Ave. Mackinaw City , MI 49701 A point in the storm and a guiding beacon since 1889, Old Mackinac Point Lighthouse helped passing ships navigate through the treacherous waters of the Straits of Mackinac.
