Suhosin PHP hardening project Suhosin-NG The next generation PHP hardening project About Suhosin-NG Suhosin (pronounced 'su-ho-shin') is an advanced protection system for PHP installations. It is designed to protect servers and users from known and unknown flaws in PHP applications and the PHP core. Suhosin-NG is heavily based on improving the Snuffleupagus PHP extension. Development Status (2021) Suhosin-NG is a work in progress. Even though there may be only few updates to our forked Snuffleupagus Github repository , there is a lot going on behind the curtains. For more updates, please check out our News Section and our Twitter Feed . Old Suhosin resources Documentation regarding the old Suhosin for PHP 5 moved to suhosin5.suhosin.org . Who is behind Suhosin-NG? The Susohin-NG project was initiated by SektionEins GmbH , an IT Security Company based in Bonn/Germany.
