What are all the CSGO Game modes?

Here are all the CS2 game modes:

Case Battles: Players compete by opening CS:GO cases; the one with the highest total value of skins wins the whole pot.
Double: You stake on colors, and a multiplier determines your payout if you win.
Upgrader: Try to upgrade your current skins to higher value skins, odds based on the difference.
Mines: Inspired by Minesweeper, avoid the mines for a bigger multiplier payout.
Plinko: Drop a ball and stake on which slot it lands for a chance at big multipliers.
Roll: Stake if a Roll is above or below a certain number for quick payouts.
Case Openings: The classic case opening format where you can unbox new skins.
Bomb Rush: Successive case openings increase the potential payout, but hitting a bad one resets it.
