BitDegree is a gamified and incentive-driven (Play-and-Earn) platform for learning about crypto and the wider Web3 space. Players participate in topic- or project-dedicated BitDegree Missions, where they solve quizzes, tackle blockchain & social actions, and research topics from provided content pieces - all within a set time limit. While the learning happens in the background, participants can earn crypto learning rewards and win unique prizes! How can I earn crypto rewards? You can learn and earn crypto rewards by completing Missions on BitDegree . Each Mission comes equipped with its own unique rewards, ranging from tokens, airdrops and NFTs , all the way to tangible rewards, such as merch or hardware wallets . Completing BitDegree Missions, you will also earn Bits - points that can then be exchanged for tokens or exclusive crypto rewards. Earn more Bits by inviting your friends over to participate in Missions! What kind of crypto rewards can I get? The crypto rewards received from BitDegree Missions play-to-earn game will vary depending on the Mission you're solving. All Missions grant a specific number of Bits upon completion - these are points that can later be exchanged for exclusive rewards. Free Mission crypto rewards themselves can include tokens, stablecoins, NFTs, or even tangible goodies, such as hardware crypto wallets, tickets to exclusive events, and major project merchandise! What are Bits? Bits are special points used in the BitDegree Missions . Players can accumulate Bits in a few ways - by successfully completing Mission rounds, by completing the Mission in full, by reaching certain Streak milestones, and by inviting their friends over to participate in Missions & learn and earn crypto together! The more Bits you have, the higher your Degree will be - a higher Degree results in better odds of winning crypto rewards! What is a Mission Builder? The BitDegree Mission Builder is a dedicated tool for creating "Learn and Earn" crypto Missions . It's a custom-developed, fully functional user interface that will guide you every step of the way, from setting up your Mission visuals and free crypto rewards info, all the way to creating unique rounds comprised of different tasks to complete. The Mission Builder lends itself to being the perfect way to create a dedicated P2E game for your community! What are Guilds? BitDegree Guilds are project-dedicated spaces within the BitDegree Missions platform. Each Guild houses Play-to-Earn Missions about the underlying platform, which makes them a great way to learn Web3 and find out more about your favorite projects . For creators, Guilds serve as a community-building tool - a place where you can educate your fans about your project, offer unique Mission experiences, and reach a broader audience.
