Transitioning to New Stewardship After 52 Years

After more than five decades in the dance community, Discount Dance is beginning an exciting new chapter. We are transitioning to new ownership and preparing to move to a new platform. As part of this transition and to maintain the integrity of our service channels, the Discount Dance website will pause taking new orders at this time.

All orders placed before 7am Pacific on 12/2 will be processed and shipped by 12/15.

From studios to stages, recitals to rehearsals, it has been our honor to support your dance journey. Thank you for dancing with us for the last 52 years. Your loyalty, passion, and artistry have meant everything to us, and will continue to inspire this next chapter.

Members of our Studio Owner and Schools & Business Programs may continue to order products here on Discountdance.com by using your Discount Dance login credentials. You may also contact your dedicated Account Manager to request My Logowear products moving forward.

With gratitude,
The Discount Dance Family
