Top Currency Pairs (December 2025)

Currency pairs, also known as forex, measure the value of one currency against another. Currencies on the FX market are always traded in pairs. In order to find out the relative value of one currency, you need another currency to compare. A currency pair is the quotation of the relative value of a currency unit against the unit of another currency in the foreign exchange market. The currency that is used as the reference is called the counter currency, quote currency, and the currency that is quoted in relation is called the base currency or transaction currency. Forex is the largest and most volatile market in the world with hundreds of currency combinations to choose from. To simplify things, here are the ten most traded forex pairs on the market.
