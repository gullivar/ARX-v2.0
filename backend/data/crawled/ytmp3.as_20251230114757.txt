How to use YTMP3?
Simply go to YouTube and search for a video you would like to download. Copy the video URL from your browser's address bar. Please note: A valid video URL has the following format: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=videoId.
Paste the video URL into our converter above. Then select a download format. You can choose between MP3 (audio) or MP4 (video). If you do not choose a format, the video will be converted to an MP3 file by default. Click the Convert button.
The conversion will start. Depending on the length of the video, the conversion may take some time. As soon as the conversion is finished successfully, you will be able to download the converted file by clicking the Download button.
